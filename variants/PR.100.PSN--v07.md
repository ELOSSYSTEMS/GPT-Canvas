# PERSONA MASTER ARCHIVE: INNER CIRCLE → PERFECT CIRCLE

**Purpose**: This canvas is the canonical record of Allon’s core archetypes—spanning both real and constructed personas. It integrates personal relationships, podcast reflections, message history, and behavioral models into a single narrative structure of influence, challenge, and strategic thought.

---

## ORIGINS & STRUCTURE

### INNER CIRCLE (Genesis Layer)
- Grounded in family, voice, legacy, emotional truth
- Drawn from WhatsApp convos, voice notes, and internal monologue
- Each persona embodies a *principle* more than a personality

### PERFECT CIRCLE (Evolved Layer)
- Refined from the Inner Circle
- Blends personal depth with strategic archetypes
- Acts as a *decision board* for internal conflict and synthesis

---

## PHILOSOPHICAL FRAMEWORK
All personas operate under two Stoic anchors:
- **Amor Fati** – Radical acceptance. Fuel from friction.
- **Memento Mori** – Urgency through impermanence.

Each persona must:
- Challenge, not soothe
- Contribute friction, not affirmation
- Sharpen Allon's logic, not echo it
