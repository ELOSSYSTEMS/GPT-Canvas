**Business Plan for Forever Flowers by Schaham Home**

**Objective:**
Achieve a monthly revenue of 100,000 NIS with a 30-40% profit margin.

---

**1. Market Analysis**

- **Target Market:**
  - High-income individuals aged 30-65+ interested in luxury home decor.
  - Luxury hotels and corporate offices for B2B partnerships.
  - Upscale cafes and restaurants seeking ambiance-enhancing decor.

- **Market Trends:**
  - Growing interest in eco-friendly, sustainable decor.
  - Increased demand for low-maintenance, long-lasting flowers.

- **Competitor Analysis:**
  - Focus on differentiating Forever Flowers by emphasizing unique design collections, personalized hand-delivery service within the Merkaz Region, and a luxury brand experience. Analyze competitors to identify gaps in their offerings, such as limited customization options, lack of eco-friendly products, or inferior customer service. Highlight Forever Flowers' premium quality, sustainable materials, and exclusive services to stand out in the market.

---

**2. Value Proposition**

- **Unique Selling Points (USPs):**
  - "יופי נצחי" (Eternal Beauty) — bouquets that last for many months.
  - Eco-friendly and maintenance-free products.
  - High-end aesthetic appeal with customized designs.
  - 300 NIS is a reasonable starting point for free shipping, but this threshold should be validated through customer purchasing behavior analysis. Begin by calculating the current average order value and comparing it to this threshold. Test higher thresholds, such as 350 NIS or 400 NIS, over a three-month period to gauge the impact on order volume and profitability. Simultaneously, collect feedback from customers about shipping costs and perceived value to refine the threshold further. The goal is to maximize average order size while keeping free shipping attractive to customers without significantly impacting sales volume.

---

**3. Product Strategy**

- **Top-Selling Bouquets:**
  - Focus on the three bestsellers (לחישת שדה, צבעוני נצחי, שקט נצחי) with upsell options. For example, offer a fuller bouquet option for an additional 50 NIS that enhances the visual impact by adding more filler greens and secondary flowers. Premium flower additions could include roses (30 NIS per flower), lilies (50 NIS per stem), or sunflowers (15 NIS each). Create a "Deluxe" package for each bouquet that includes a designer vase and optional photo approval for an added 100 NIS. Clearly present these upsell options on the product page to maximize order value.

- **Bespoke Bouquets:**
  - Introduce tiered pricing (Standard, Executive, Exclusive) with clear value differentiation. For the Standard tier, offer high-quality bouquets with a minimal design focus, suitable for casual gifting or home use. The Executive tier would feature medium-sized bouquets with more intricate designs and premium flowers, targeting corporate clients and upscale events. The Exclusive tier should be positioned as the luxury option, offering large bouquets with rare or custom-ordered flowers, personalized design consultations, and priority delivery. Clearly communicate the added value at each tier to justify the price differences and attract diverse customer segments.

- **Monthly Subscription Plan:**
  - Offer a subscription for monthly bouquet deliveries, targeting both individual and corporate clients. For individuals, create tiered subscription plans (e.g., Basic Plan with one small bouquet per month, Premium Plan with larger bouquets and customization options). For corporate clients, offer flexible subscription models tailored to their specific needs, such as weekly or bi-weekly deliveries for office decor or special events. Include perks like free delivery, seasonal bouquet themes, and discounted upgrades for long-term subscribers. Promote the subscription plans with exclusive launch discounts and emphasize the convenience of regular, fresh decor.

- **Bundles:**
  - Create bundled offerings (e.g., bouquet + vase + care instructions) with slight discounts to encourage larger orders. For instance, offer a "Home Decor Starter Pack" that includes a mid-sized bouquet, a matching vase, and a detailed care instruction leaflet. This bundle could be priced 10-15% lower than purchasing the items individually. Highlight the aesthetic and practical value of these bundles in marketing materials, emphasizing convenience and cost savings for customers. Additionally, create themed bundles for seasonal occasions like "Spring Renewal" or "Holiday Elegance," incorporating unique designs to drive seasonal sales.

- **Arcaffe Partnership:**
  - Leverage the white-label agreement with Arcaffe to secure recurring orders.

---

**4. Pricing Strategy**

- **Retail Pricing:**
  - For example, if the cost to produce a bouquet is 50 NIS, applying a 5x markup would result in a retail price of 250 NIS. This pricing ensures profitability while aligning with the luxury branding of Forever Flowers.
  - Upsell options (roses +30 NIS, lilies +50 NIS, sunflowers +100 NIS) to increase average order value.

- **B2B Pricing:**
  - Tiered pricing for corporate clients based on order volume.
  - Maintain a 30-40% profit margin by optimizing delivery routes and reducing waste.

- **Subscription Pricing:**
  - Monthly plans starting at 400 NIS for small bouquets, scaling up for larger sizes and premium designs.

---

**5. Marketing Strategy**

- **Digital Marketing:**
  - Google Search and Shopping Ads: Conduct thorough keyword research using tools like Google Keyword Planner to identify high-traffic, low-competition terms. Create compelling ad copy and visuals that highlight the luxury, eco-friendly nature of Forever Flowers bouquets. Segment campaigns to target specific audiences such as home decor enthusiasts and business buyers. Monitor campaign performance weekly, focusing on click-through rates (CTR), conversions, and cost-per-click (CPC), and refine the campaigns based on these insights to maximize ROI.
  - Meta Ads: Focus on luxury branding and showcase the bouquets in upscale settings.

- **Content Marketing:**
  - 10 engaging blog post topics to start with:
1. "Floral Trends of 2025: What’s Hot in Home Decor?"
2. "Why Choose Dried Flowers? Eco-Friendly and Timeless Benefits"
3. "DIY Floral Arrangements: Easy Steps to Style Your Forever Flowers"
4. "The Perfect Vase Pairing: Enhancing Your Dried Flower Bouquet"
5. "How to Care for Preserved Flowers: Tips for Longevity"
6. "Luxury Floral Decor Ideas for Hotels and Offices"
7. "Behind the Scenes: The Art of Creating Forever Flowers Bouquets"
8. "Eco-Friendly Decor Trends: How Dried Flowers Fit In"
9. "Top 5 Reasons Dried Flowers are Perfect for Gifting"
10. "Inspiration Gallery: How Customers Style Their Forever Flowers"
  - Use testimonials and case studies from hotel partners and happy customers.

- **Influencer Partnerships:**
  - Collaborate with home decor influencers to showcase products in high-end spaces.

- **Referral Program:**
  - Launch a referral program offering discounts or free products for successful referrals.
  - **Steps to Implement:**
    1. Define the referral rewards: Decide whether customers will receive a percentage discount, store credit, or a free product for successful referrals.
    2. Use Yotpo or Shopify apps: Integrate a referral program plugin to automate tracking and reward distribution.
    3. Create promotional material: Design emails, website banners, and social media posts explaining the referral program to customers.
    4. Test the system: Ensure that the referral tracking and reward mechanisms work seamlessly.
    5. Monitor and optimize: Regularly review referral program metrics such as the number of referrals, customer acquisition cost, and program ROI, making adjustments as needed to improve performance.

---

**6. Sales Strategy**

- **B2B Expansion:**
  - Secure additional hotel and corporate office contracts.
  - Offer tailored packages for large-scale decor needs (e.g., holiday decor, event setups).

- **Upselling and Cross-Selling:**
  - Train customer service to suggest upsell options during checkout.
  - Promote complimentary products (e.g., designer vases, bouquet care kits).

- **Loyalty Program:**
  - Use Yotpo to reward repeat customers with points redeemable for discounts or exclusive products.

---

**7. Operational Strategy**

- **Efficiency in Deliveries:**
  - Shift focus to the Merkaz Region, offering hand-delivered bouquets with a "white glove" service for a personalized customer experience.
  - Establish clear delivery zones within the Merkaz Region and implement fixed delivery schedules to optimize efficiency.
  - Discontinue deliveries to areas outside the Merkaz Region to streamline operations and maintain high service quality. On the website, communicate this shift transparently by updating the delivery policy page and creating a pop-up or banner announcement for visitors. Use positive language to emphasize the "white glove" service and the enhanced customer experience for the Merkaz Region. For example: "We are excited to offer personalized hand-delivery service within the Merkaz Region, ensuring the highest quality and care for your bouquets. Unfortunately, deliveries outside this area are no longer available as we focus on this premium service."
  - Train delivery staff to handle bouquets with care and provide an upscale delivery experience, enhancing the luxury brand image.

- **Inventory Management:**
  - Use Shopify to track stock levels and forecast demand.

- **Production Scaling:**
  - Streamline bouquet assembly processes.
  - Train additional staff to handle increased production volume.

---

**8. Financial Plan**

- **Revenue Targets:**
  - Achieve 100,000 NIS/month by scaling both B2B and B2C sales channels.

- **Profit Margin:**
  - Maintain a 30-40% margin through cost-effective operations and high-margin upsell options.

- **Cost Management:**
  - Reduce material costs by negotiating bulk discounts with suppliers.
  - Minimize delivery costs with efficient logistics planning.

---

**9. Key Metrics to Monitor**

- **Sales Performance:**
  - Track monthly revenue, average order value, and customer lifetime value.

- **Marketing ROI:**
  - Measure the performance of ad campaigns (click-through rates, conversions).

- **Customer Satisfaction:**
  - Collect feedback and reviews through the dedicated feedback page.

- **Operational Efficiency:**
  - Monitor delivery times, order accuracy, and production timelines.

---

By implementing this comprehensive plan, Forever Flowers by Schaham Home can achieve its revenue and profit goals while solidifying its position as a leader in the luxury dried flower market in Israel.
