## 🔹 Why, How, What

### Why Does Forever Flowers Exist?
> "To change the way people experience flowers — from temporary to timeless, from wasteful to meaningful."

### How Do We Do That?
- Crafting elegant, sustainable preserved and dried floral arrangements.
- Providing customization to match the emotion of each customer.
- Offering beauty that requires no maintenance and lasts.

### What Do We Actually Do?
- Sell preserved flower bouquets online.
- Serve B2B clients (cafés, hotels, offices) with floral decor.
- Build custom floral art pieces for special events.
