### FF.700.ARC – Arcaffe Shavuot Delivery Master

#### Purpose
This canvas documents the full operational plan for coordinating, preparing, and delivering Shavuot floral orders for Arcaffe, including workflow timings, client communication, bouquet production, and delivery logistics. It is designed to minimize risk, avoid last-minute bottlenecks, and ensure B2B reliability.

---

#### 📦 Order Overview
- **Client:** Arcaffe (B2B)
- **Occasion:** Shavuot (high-volume, high-visibility event)
- **Products Ordered:** Premium dried flower bouquets in branded wrapping
- **Order Size:** ~50–100 units
- **Delivery Mode:** Direct owner delivery

---

#### 🗓 Timeline
| Phase | Deadline | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| Confirm Order | May 1 | Include signed PO or email confirmation |
| Begin Production Prep | May 3 | Pre-select dried stems, allocate team time |
| Finalize Wrapping Supplies | May 5 | Branded sleeves, twine, inserts |
| Start Assembly | May 7 | Daily limit: ~20 units (30 mins/bouquet) |
| Final QA Check | May 10 | Verify freshness, packing, uniformity |
| Delivery Date | May 11 | Staged by route, grouped by location |

---

#### 🛠 Production Logic
- **Staff:** Shiran (lead maker), assistant if available
- **Average Time per Unit:** 30 minutes
- **Workload Distribution:** 4–5 hours/day over 4–5 days
- **Inventory Prep:**
  - Use mostly pre-dried stock
  - Ensure redundancy (10% extra bundles)

---

#### 🚚 Delivery Plan
- **Driver:** Allon (owner/operator)
- **Route Optimization:** Built in Google Maps + WhatsApp delivery list
- **Timing:** Early morning before café opening hours where possible
- **Backup:** Renter’s van or friend’s Sprinter if volume exceeds car space

#### 🔒 Contingency & Risks
- **Risk 1:** Production Overload → Mitigation: Cut order at 80%, communicate clearly
- **Risk 2:** Delay in Wrapping Supplies → Mitigation: Keep 1 week buffer stock
- **Risk 3:** Last-Minute Changes → Mitigation: Written lock-in by May 3

---

#### 🔁 Lessons Learned from 2024
- Add branded stickers to each bouquet (missed last year)
- Reduce over-wrapping; cafes complained about too much paper
- Delivery before noon ensures best placement
