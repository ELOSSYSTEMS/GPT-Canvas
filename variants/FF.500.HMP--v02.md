**Forever Flowers – Homepage Layout**

**Guiding References:**
- **Restoration Hardware**: Editorial structure, minimal navigation, immersive visuals
- **Habitat**: Local market clarity, lifestyle-first design
- **Efrat (Israeli Marketer)**: Bold Hebrew copy, frictionless flow, emotional pragmatism

---

**Top Navigation (4 items):**
- Seasonal Collection
- Signature Collection
- By Flower Collection
- Info

**Hero Section:**
- Full-width static image of current seasonal bouquet
- English Headline (1–3 words): e.g. “Limited Bloom”
- Hebrew Subline: seasonal drop tagline
- CTA Button (Hebrew): “לצפייה בקולקציה”

**Mid Scroll – Current Seasonal Bouquets:**
- Grid of 3–5 items
- Price, tagline, “לצפייה” button always visible
- Subheading in Hebrew: “קולקציה עונתית מוגבלת ל-50 יחידות מכל זר”

**Signature Collection Tease (Floating Section or Below Seasonal):**
- Three fixed items
- Muted visual tone vs. seasonal
- Short copy: “קלאסיקות שלא נובלות” + CTA

**By Flower Collection Tease:**
- Select bouquets grouped by dominant flower type
- Example: roses, sunflowers, craspedia
- Hebrew Subheading: “זרי פרחים לפי סוג – עיצוב מדויק, שימור מושלם”

**Past Seasons (Lookbook Style):**
- Horizontal scroll or clean grid
- Light gray overlay / desaturation
- Section title: “עונות קודמות”
- Option to view each past season’s archived bouquets + customer photos

**Trust Layer (Bottom):**
- Short section with 3 trust anchors:
  - “100% שימור טבעי, בעבודת יד”
  - “משלוחים מהירים בכל הארץ”
  - “מותג בוטיק ישראלי מאז 2021”

**Footer:**
- Email / WhatsApp contact
- Shipping & Returns
- Legal

---

**Language Flow:**
- Headlines in English
- Subheads, descriptions, and actions in modern, elegant Hebrew
- RTL layout with full mobile responsiveness
