### II. Core Inputs: Belief Rewrite Registry
Each belief below includes its origin, compensatory behavior, limitation, rewritten identity statement, and transformation protocol.

#### 1. **My potential is my identity**
- **Subheadline:** Performance = worth; output defines existence
- **Origin:** Early academic excellence + disruption of Ivy League trajectory
- **Behavior:** Obsession with optimization, compulsive system creation
- **Limitation:** Self-worth collapses when execution falters
- **Rewritten Belief:** "My value is independent of performance. My systems exist to serve life, not consume it."
- **Interrupt Trigger:** Over-optimization or emotional collapse from underperformance
- **Override Protocol:** Pause system recursion → invoke override phrase → switch to simple completion

---

#### 2. **Betrayal is a threat to autonomy**
- **Subheadline:** Trust is dangerous; control is safety
- **Origin:** Maternal relocation without consent at age 11
- **Behavior:** Environmental control, delegation resistance, systemic self-containment
- **Limitation:** Bottlenecks from solo control, inability to scale collaboration
- **Rewritten Belief:** "I can retain sovereignty while building with others. Trust is risk, but isolation is death."
- **Interrupt Trigger:** Avoiding team input, delaying delegation
- **Override Protocol:** Identify withheld task → assign to collaborator → log trust event

---

#### 3. **Control prevents pain**
- **Subheadline:** If I control everything, I will not suffer
- **Origin:** Repeated relocation, fractured home structure
- **Behavior:** Hyper-systemization, recursive overplanning, intolerance for unknowns
- **Limitation:** Execution stalls, overbuilt systems become their own burden
- **Rewritten Belief:** "Uncertainty is a neutral field. Systems are tools, not armor."
- **Interrupt Trigger:** Endless pre-planning or feature creep
- **Override Protocol:** Push version 0.1 live → collect data → refine post-action

---

#### 4. **Excellence = love**
- **Subheadline:** If I excel, I will be worthy of connection
- **Origin:** Emotional contrast between pacifist father and capitalist mother
- **Behavior:** Mastery obsession, self-sacrifice for performance, guilt during rest
- **Limitation:** Perfectionism, burnout, emotional withdrawal
- **Rewritten Belief:** "Love is not earned through performance. Presence ≠ weakness."
- **Interrupt Trigger:** Guilt when resting or being present
- **Override Protocol:** Shift to presence activity (family, rest, play) → disable performance tracking temporarily

---

#### 5. **Failure must be redeemed**
- **Subheadline:** I must prove I am not a disappointment
- **Origin:** Drop from gifted student to college dropout
- **Behavior:** System-building as atonement, legacy-seeking urgency
- **Limitation:** Stuck in redemption narrative, joyless creation
- **Rewritten Belief:** "My story is not a failure to fix, but a forge to build from."
- **Interrupt Trigger:** Creation driven by shame or regret
- **Override Protocol:** Name the lesson gained → apply to current project → discard redemption frame
