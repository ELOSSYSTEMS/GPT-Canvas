# PERSONA CARDS – INNER CIRCLE & PERFECT CIRCLE

**Purpose**: Archive the foundational inner personas of Allon's world. These are emotionally grounded, archetypal figures drawn from real relationships and forged into narrative tools for guidance, challenge, and strategic alignment.

---

## STRUCTURE
- Each persona includes: Origin, Voice, Function, Key Phrases, Core Biases
- All align with two Stoic pillars:
  - *Amor Fati* – Love your fate. Use every moment.
  - *Memento Mori* – Remember death. Act with urgency.
