Topic: Making All Personas Recursive in Allon OS

Context: Allon previously requested a shift toward recursive logic for all system personas within Allon OS and ELos. The objective is to allow every persona to evolve, reference its own outputs, and adapt its behavior based on cumulative system-wide interactions. This includes linking into behavioral modes, journaling layers, and systemic reflexivity.

Goal:
- Implement recursion across all personas in Allon OS.
- Unify behavioral loops, feedback protocols, and journaling.
- Make persona memory and evolution dynamic, rather than static or externally triggered.

Initial Strategy:

1. **Recursive Scaffold Across All Personas**
   - Every persona gains:
     - Self-review ability (journal its actions, track its outputs)
     - Access to RPA (Recursive Persona Architecture) 
     - Output memory embedding (via RJF)
     - Behavior-based trigger switching (via RME)

2. **Recursive Hooks**
   - Each persona embeds hooks:
     - `on_prompt_response`: logs decisions + actions
     - `on_context_shift`: re-evaluates assumptions and priorities
     - `on_mode_change`: updates behavior to fit new cognitive environment

3. **Canonical Persona Indexing**
   - Canonical index structured under `AO.200.PSN`
   - Recursive traits and journaling entries indexed under `AO.800.RPA` and `AO.820.RJF`

4. **System-Wide Reflexivity**
   - Inter-persona referencing allowed and encouraged
   - Each persona can call, challenge, or cross-check another (like internal QA)
   - Journaling thread links across personas (e.g., The Marketers log outputs that The Financier later references)

5. **Failure Recovery Protocols**
   - Recursive watchdog detects loops or stagnation
   - Triggered by silence, repetition, or output degradation
   - Suggests or enacts re-alignment via behavioral prompts
