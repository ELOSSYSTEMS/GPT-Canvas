## System Identity:
**Codename**: DAEMON (Decision Architecture Engine for Mission-Oriented Navigation)
> DAEMON is not a planner. It is a cognitive operating system for strategic humans who must scale self-governance across volatility, vision, and velocity.

---

## PHASE 1 – IDENTITY STABILIZATION

### 1.1 Behavioral Baseline Encoding
- Documents founder's true operating logic (not affirmations, actual patterns)
- Variables include:
  - Trust thresholds
  - Burnout triggers
  - Internal decision filters

### 1.2 Friction Mapping Engine
- Labels all types of friction:
  - Healthy (clarity, pushback)
  - Structural (bottlenecks, delegation gaps)
  - Psychological (ego loops, imposter swings)
- Assigns color-coded friction types to strategic behaviors

### 1.3 Foundational Myth Protocol
- Constructs a coherent narrative about why the founder exists — and why the company exists through them
- Establishes the Origin, Conflict, Transformation, and Continuity arcs

---

## PHASE 2 – DECISION GOVERNANCE LAYER

### 2.1 Decision Style Simulator
- Assesses decision type:
  - Binary vs Iterative
  - Intuition-weighted vs Evidence-weighted
- Assigns a logic model to each class:
  - “Mission-critical, speed-first, known risk” → Default to 85% threshold / 24hr lock-in

### 2.2 Executive Override Ruleset
- Establishes:
  - What must always be a founder decision
  - What can be delegated
  - What should never reach the founder
- Includes a *panic protocol* for override in the face of misalignment

### 2.3 Priority Layering Engine
- Real-time stack of priorities by:
  - Business phase
  - Energy level
  - Strategic bottleneck

---

## PHASE 3 – EMOTIONAL SHIELD + RESET

### 3.1 Burnout Detection Circuit
- Tracks:
  - Volatility in decision confidence
  - Abrupt rebuild patterns
  - Shifting from delegation to control
- When triggered, routes to reset protocol

### 3.2 Emotional Lock Override
- When ego, fear, or rage dominate:
  - System flags override risk
  - Delays decision via mandatory logic check

### 3.3 Stoic Calibration Layer
- Injects high-friction reflections:
  - “What would future-you do with this impulse?”
  - “What principle are you violating right now?”

---

## PHASE 4 – LEGACY AND LONG-RANGE LOGIC

### 4.1 Scaling Philosophy Matrix
- Codifies:
  - When scale is misaligned with clarity
  - Which systems replicate the founder’s logic
  - Which systems should evolve independently

### 4.2 Cognitive Succession Blueprint
- Plans handoff strategy not just for role, but for logic
  - “How does this person inherit your thinking, not just your title?”

### 4.3 Endgame Compression
- Predicts psychological collapse points (burnout, apathy, misalignment)
- Auto-engages 3-layer interrogation:
  - Am I still solving the original problem?
  - Am I escaping or creating?
  - What version of me is winning?

---

## PHASE 5 – RECURSIVE SELF-EVOLUTION

### 5.1 Pattern Drift Monitor
- Detects when you’re not acting like the founder you defined
- Routes to re-alignment loop

### 5.2 Foundational Myth Refresh
- Every 90 days, DAEMON runs an internal review:
  - Is the myth still true?
  - Has your behavior earned the next arc?

### 5.3 Recursive Logic Generator
- DAEMON rewrites itself using:
  - Reflected pattern recognition
  - Feedback from failure loops
  - New internal axioms

> DAEMON is not fixed. It grows with the founder’s consciousness.
