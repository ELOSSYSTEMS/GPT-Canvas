## 🎭 Emergent Aesthetic Codes from Israeli Luxury Interiors:

### 1. **Palettes: Dust Over Contrast**
- Dominant tones: cream, bone, light oak, sand, matte slate
- Accents: terracotta, mustard, oxidized bronze, forest green, powder pink
- **Rule**: Vibrancy is always softened by time or earth.

### 2. **Materials: Raw + Refined**
- Surfaces: plaster, poured concrete, stone cladding, brushed oak
- Furnishings: fluted wood, woven linen, handmade ceramics
- **Rule**: Imperfection signals craft, not cost-cutting.

### 3. **Spatial Flow: Quiet Geometry**
- Rooms prioritize natural paths, open flow, framed sightlines
- Use of repetition: square beams, ribbed ceilings, sunken nooks
- **Rule**: Every scene is an invitation to slow down

### 4. **Lighting: Natural with Emphasis**
- Primary: Indirect natural light from courtyards, high windows, deep eaves
- Secondary: Concealed LEDs, wall sconces, pendant soft orbs
- **Rule**: Light is sculpted, not exposed

### 5. **Decor Philosophy: Functional Restraint**
- Maximal 3 objects per major focal surface
- Centerpieces (like floral arrangements) are sculptural, never ornamental
- **Rule**: Everything must have mass, not clutter
