### 🧠 Prompt Processing Stack
All prompts processed through this canvas can be routed into:

- 🔬 **Stargate Protocol (v3.1-total-fireproofed)**
- 🧠 **Prompt Surgeon**: structural rewrite, tone fix, ambiguity reduction
- 🧪 **Consistency Auditor**: tone, constraint, format, verb alignment
- 🛡️ **Fireproofing Layer**: validation against hallucination, recursion, overreach
- 🔄 **Post-Processing Chains**: constraint enforcement, self-debug
