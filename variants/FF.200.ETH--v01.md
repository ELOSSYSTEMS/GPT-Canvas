# FOREVER FLOWERS – BRAND ETHOS & LEXICON

**Purpose**: Define the voice, values, and linguistic DNA of the Forever Flowers brand. Serves as the compass for all copywriting, customer-facing content, and internal creative alignment.

---

## CORE ETHOS

- **Premium doesn’t mean cold** – It’s warm, human, intimate.
- **Not quiet. Not loud. Intentional.** – Each bouquet makes a statement, never noise.
- **Crafted, not produced** – Real hands, real artistry, no AI-fluff or factory filler.
- **Slowness is a virtue** – 10-day delivery is intentional, not inefficient.
- **Meaning > Occasion** – Flowers that mark emotion, not just events.

---

## SIGNATURE LANGUAGE

- "זר בעבודת יד. לא מפס ייצור."
- "עיצוב שמחזיק שנים – כמו הרגש שבחרת לבטא."
- "אמן שוזר. לא שליח."
- "לא מהיר. מדויק."
- "משלוח פרימיום שמוגש ביד, לא בקופסה."

---

## DESIGN VOCABULARY

- **Color Palette**: Olive green, bone, dark natural paper, ivory accents
- **Materials**: Kraft paper wrap optional (must align with Atelier tone), no plastic
- **Typography & Layout**: Clean, serif + sans-serif blend, soft shadows, no clutter

---

## TONE
- Native Israeli Hebrew: real, flowing, non-formal
- Zero cliché
- No ‘מלבלב’, no ‘קסום’. Avoid empty floral adjectives.
- Think RH meets indie Tel Aviv studio
