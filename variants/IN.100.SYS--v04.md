**GPTOS – Strategic Blind Spots & Pre-Build Alignment**

**Purpose:** Identify critical strategic questions before entering the full build phase of GPTOS. These blind spots are addressed with aligned responses from Allon to clarify direction, monetization, UX philosophy, and user protection mechanisms.

---

**I. Monetization Model**
- **Blind Spot:** How should tiers be priced and structured?
- **Allon’s Direction:**
  - **Pro Tier:** Lifetime one-time purchase (justifies higher price).
  - **Pro+ Tier:** Monthly subscription due to API and sync features.
  - **Free Tier:** Powerful but intentionally impermanent — sticky, yet restrictive.

---

**II. Product Positioning**
- **Blind Spot:** Is GPTOS a prompt enhancer or something bigger?
- **Allon’s Direction:** GPTOS is a **power user operating system for ChatGPT**.
  - Appeals to advanced users.
  - Still accessible to beginners via onboarding, tooltips, and progressive UX.

---

**III. Aha Moment Clarity**
- **Blind Spot:** When does the user experience “this is powerful”?
- **Allon’s Direction:**
  - First 60 seconds = onboarding tour
  - Aha moment engineered via early interaction with: 
    - Mode toggle confirmation
    - Journaling module
    - Canvas access simulation
  - Power users will recognize the depth by exploring behavior layers

---

**IV. Privacy & Legal Readiness**
- **Blind Spot:** What are the data risks and legal obligations?
- **Allon’s Direction:**
  - All storage defaults to local only.
  - Cloud sync (Pro+) is opt-in with transparent terms.
  - All handling will comply with GDPR and Israeli data protection law.
  - Clear TOS and Privacy Policy will be visible during onboarding.
  - Sensitive journaling actions will always include consent reminders.

---

**V. Chrome Store Compliance**
- **Blind Spot:** Could injection or behavior cause rejection?
- **Allon’s Direction:**
  - Manifest and injection methods will follow all best practices.
  - Branding will explicitly state “not affiliated with OpenAI.”
  - All UI modifications will be toggleable and isolated.
  - Store meta copy will clarify all data use and extension behavior.

---

**VI. Data Corruption & Recovery Protocol**
- **Blind Spot:** What happens if localStorage is corrupted?
- **Allon’s Direction:**
  - Notify user if local corruption is detected.
  - Request user to re-import saved data.
  - Future plan: Implement internal backup utility with optional recurring backups (manual or scheduled).
