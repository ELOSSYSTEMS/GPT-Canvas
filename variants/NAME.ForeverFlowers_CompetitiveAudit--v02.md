**Forever Flowers – Competitive Landscape & Strategic Growth Audit**

---

### 1. COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE

#### Direct Competitors (Israel-based)

**1. Kimaya Flowers**  
- **Positioning**: Eco-conscious, artisanal dried flower brand using locally grown flowers. Appeals to design-savvy customers who want sustainable beauty for home/events.  
- **Tone**: Warm, natural, handmade aesthetic.  
- **Pricing**: ₪165–₪415 for full bouquets (avg. ₪250–₪300); ₪85–₪260 for smaller sets.  
- **Moves**: Workshops, DIY kits, B2B expansion (weddings/business decor), styled sets.

**2. Pampas Mood**  
- **Positioning**: Trendy, boho-chic decor brand centered around pampas and preserved botanicals. Targets fashion-forward home decorators and event planners.  
- **Tone**: Stylish, celebratory.  
- **Pricing**: ₪299–₪429 for bundles; up to ₪599+ for deluxe sets; ₪199 on sale.  
- **Moves**: Loyalty club, free shipping over ₪555, home accessories, event styling.

**3. Blossom Flower Boutique**  
- **Positioning**: Upscale luxury floral boutique focusing on preserved roses in elegant boxes. Trilingual (Hebrew/English/Russian), targets high-end gift givers.  
- **Tone**: Romantic, premium, VIP-focused.  
- **Pricing**: Preserved rose boxes up to ₪790; smaller box tiers likely in ₪300–₪500 range.  
- **Moves**: Celebrity-style arrangements on IG, workshops, brand collaborations (hotels, jewelry).

#### Adjacent Disruptors (Israel-based)

**1. Snappy**  
- **Space**: Corporate gifting platform using smart tech for personalized gifts at scale.  
- **Tone**: Professional, tech-empowered.  
- **Pricing**: Gifts often ₪100–₪400 value; pricing handled per corporate deal.  
- **Moves**: Acquired Covver, built AI recommendation engine, optimized ROI tracking.

**2. Bring It Home**  
- **Space**: D2C luxury home decor brand with Nordic-minimalist aesthetic.  
- **Tone**: Inspirational, curated, stylish.  
- **Pricing**: Items from ₪200–₪1,500; free shipping over ₪750.  
- **Moves**: Limited artist collabs, 48hr delivery, Instagram community-first marketing.

### 2. OPPORTUNITY GAPS

**1. Outbound B2B Sales & Partnerships**  
- No current proactive B2B sales funnel.
- Huge untapped potential in hotels, law firms, real estate agents, etc.

**2. Loyalty & Referral Programs**  
- No customer club or repeat-purchase incentives.
- Competitors already rewarding repeat buyers.

**3. Bundled Sets & Tiered Upsells**  
- Current vase add-ons are passive.
- Could package “Complete Gift Sets” with intentional design.

**4. Influencer Seeding + UGC Campaigns**  
- Very low-cost, high-impact way to build brand awareness and trust.
- UGC could help address poor Meta Ad CTR (<1%).

**5. Alternative Sales Channels**  
- Not on Wolt or any fast-delivery platforms.
- Misses out on impulse / convenience shoppers.

**6. Off-Season Product Development**  
- No themed collections or DIY offerings for down months.
- Low engagement when gifting peaks subside.

### 4. RECOMMENDATIONS – TOP 3 ACTIONS

**1. Launch an Outbound B2B Sales Program**  
**Step-by-Step Roadmap:**

**Step 1: Define Ideal B2B Segments**  
- Hotels (boutique, luxury chains)
- Office lobbies (law firms, architecture studios)
- Premium retail stores (cosmetics, fashion)
- Event planners and real estate agents (gifting)

**Step 2: Develop Collateral**  
- Branded corporate catalog (PDF & Print)
  - Include pricing tiers: per piece, bulk bundles, seasonal subscriptions
  - Visual examples: lobby arrangement, boardroom florals, VIP client gifts
- Introductory 1-pager (why Forever Flowers?)
- Sample contract terms (subscription vs. one-time)

**Step 3: Build Prospect List**  
- Start with warm contacts (e.g. Arcaffe, Fattal Hotels)
- Research Tel Aviv/Herzliya design offices, boutique hotels, agencies
- Use LinkedIn, Google Maps, and cold outreach tools to identify ~50 targets

**Step 4: Outreach Campaign**  
- Email + WhatsApp intro with attached 1-pager
- Follow-up call 48 hours later
- Offer to send a free mini arrangement as a “sample” (builds reciprocity)

**Step 5: Sales Pipeline Management**  
- Use CRM or simple Notion table to track:
  - Contact status (cold, warm, sample sent, proposal pending, won)
  - Notes from calls
  - Revenue forecast

**Step 6: Pricing & Packaging**  
- Create B2B-specific SKUs:
  - Monthly lobby plan (₪850/month)
  - Bulk holiday gift orders (10+ = 10% discount)
  - Custom note card add-ons for corporate branding

**Step 7: Fulfillment & Operations**  
- Set standard delivery days for B2B clients (e.g. Mondays)
- Pre-allocate stock weekly for B2B vs. B2C
- Use invoicing with NET terms (30 or 60 days)

**Step 8: Measure & Optimize**  
- Track conversion rate of outreach → client
- Get testimonials and photos from installs
- Quarterly review: biggest spenders, upsell opportunities

**Goal:** 10–15 active B2B clients by end of Q3 2025 with ~₪15,000+ monthly recurring income.

**2. Curate and Send Influencer Gift Kits**  
- Select 15 local micro-influencers in home design / gifting.
- Offer branded unboxing experiences to generate shares.

**3. Integrate with Wolt / Bolt + Build Marketplace Listings**  
- Prepare SKUs ready for instant delivery (₪300+ gifts).
- Add Forever Flowers to local premium gift platforms.
