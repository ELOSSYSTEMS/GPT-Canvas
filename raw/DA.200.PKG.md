**Canvas Title:** DABROO AI - Packaging & Presentation

**Purpose:**
This canvas defines the visual, verbal, and experiential packaging of Dabroo AI across all user touchpoints: Chrome Extension, Web UI, marketing funnels, and onboarding.

---

**1. Core Principles:**
- **Clarity First:** Users must immediately understand what Dabroo is and what it does, without jargon or ambiguity.
- **Power Perception:** Visuals and language must signal "pro" tools, not amateur or playful.
- **Cultural Match:** Hebrew-first tone, design, and flow must match Israeli user behavior.
- **Differentiated by UX:** The packaging *is* the product. Prompt injection, interface polish, and linguistic nuance are core features.

---

**2. Product Positioning Visuals (Hero Elements):**
- Chrome extension icon: clean, minimalist blue chat bubble symbol
- UI Accent Color: Deep electric blue (signaling clarity + strength)
- Fonts: Mix of SF Pro (system trust) and Assistant (Hebrew legibility)
- Animation Logic: Subtle, fast-loading, declarative (no cutesy transitions)

---

**3. Brand Language Style Guide:**
- **Voice:** Blunt, clever, native Israeli. Sarcasm optional, clarity mandatory.
- **Default Language:** Hebrew-first. English only when absolutely necessary.
- **Messaging Style:**
  - Start with the user's frustration
  - Then: here’s how Dabroo solves it.
  - End with: "try it now" CTA in casual Hebrew (e.g., "יאללה, תן לזה צ'")
- **Words to Avoid:** Chatbot, assistant, magic, easy
- **Words to Prefer:** שליטה, חיסכון בזמן, שלב אחד קדימה, פיצוחים חכמים

---

**4. Funnel Visuals & Tone:**
- Landing Page:
  - Screenshot of Hebrew prompt > perfect Hebrew output
  - Embedded video of real user copy/paste flow
  - CTA: Try free. No signup.
- Extension Store:
  - One-sentence promise: "עברית חלקה. תשובות מדויקות. בלי לשבור את הראש."
  - Screenshots: one ultra-clean, one showing Hebrew prompt/response flow
- Social Creative:
  - Native mobile screenshots with WhatsApp overlays
  - Meme-style text: sarcastic Hebrew > Dabroo fix version
  - Format: 1:1 and 9:16 only

---

**5. Onboarding Flow:**
- 1st prompt preloaded: "מה אפשר לשאול כאן?"
- Tooltip: "תדבר חופשי. בעברית. אני אסדר את זה."
- Optional walkthrough overlay:
  - "ככה זה עובד: תכתבו. בעברית. תקבלו תשובות שבאמת נשמעות כמו ישראלי."
  - CTA: "יאללה, תן בראש."

---

**6. Expansion-Ready Assets:**
- Web UI parity with extension
- Logo pack: light/dark, icon only, full logo
- Visual consistency across email, help center, and upgrade screen
- Language toggle logic (for future English tier)

---

**Summary:**
The packaging of Dabroo AI is not decorative. It is functional UX that conveys power, fluency, and alignment with Israeli digital behavior. From icon to language, every detail is designed to feel native, confident, and indispensable.
