# FOREVER_FLOWERS_SYSTEM_PHILOSOPHY.md

## Purpose
Explain *why* the Forever Flowers site behaves the way it does.

## Core Doctrine (Israeli Luxury)
- Luxury in Israel signals **judgment, not abundance**.
- Silence implies confidence; explanation implies insecurity.
- Defaults signal authority; options signal doubt.

## Tier Philosophy
- TRY removes risk.
- DECORATE establishes taste.
- CUSTOMIZE transfers responsibility.

Tiers are **experienced through pages**, never through conditional logic.

## Absence Is Intentional
If something is not present, it is because its presence would:
- increase effort
- invite comparison
- create emotional exposure

# FOREVER_FLOWERS_SYSTEM_BOUNDARIES.md

## Purpose
Freeze ownership so systems never bleed into each other.

## Ownership Map

### Shopify Owns
- Products, variants, pricing
- Inventory and availability
- Cart and checkout
- Customer identity (baseline)

### CMS (Sanity) Owns
- Pages and editorial content
- Portfolio and journal
- Service positioning
- Tier ownership per page

### Next.js Owns
- Routing
- Rendering
- Performance
- Enforcement of tier rules

### Forever Circle (Future)
- Rewards ledger
- Community state
- Subscription logic

## Non-Negotiables
- No duplication of product truth
- No CMS control over pricing
- No Shopify control over content hierarchy

# FOREVER_FLOWERS_HOMEPAGE_AUTHORITY.md

## Purpose
Define the homepage as a routing instrument, not a sales surface.

## Homepage Job
- Orient
- Normalize
- Route

## Allowed Sections
- Single-sentence hero
- “How People Usually Start”
- Home vs Business gateway
- Limited featured collections
- One trust anchor

## Forbidden Sections
- Testimonials
- Long copy
- Process explanation
- Promotions
- Editorial storytelling

## Copy Rules
- Descriptive, not expressive
- No aspiration language
- No urgency

## Interaction Rules
- No forced CTAs
- No arrows or instructional UI
- Calm progression only
