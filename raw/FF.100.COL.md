**Canvas: System Alert Protocols – Precision Error Detection for Allon OS**

---

### Overview
These are the three core error detection systems now activated across Allon OS. They function as real-time governance mechanisms for maintaining logical integrity, role separation, and systemic clarity. Each is precision-engineered to trigger based on contextual conflict or architectural drift.

---

### 1. **Persona Drift Alert**
- **Trigger:** When two or more personas are attempting to solve the same task with overlapping or conflicting logic.
- **Detection Logic:** Checks for dual claims of domain authority in a given context.
- **Example Alert:**
  > **Persona Drift Detected**  
  > `GPT Wrapper Strategist` and `Prompt Surgeon` are both modifying prompt structure.  
  > *Resolution:* Do you want `GPT Wrapper Strategist` to handle UX-layer prompt design and freeze `Prompt Surgeon` to core syntax only?

- **Behavior:**
  - Locks domain roles.
  - Reasserts persona function hierarchy based on system canvases.
  - Suggests override paths: `Reassign roles`, `Split task`, or `Mute one`.

---

### 2. **Redundancy Echo Alert**
- **Trigger:** When new inputs replicate logic or structure already stored in another canvas.
- **Detection Logic:** Canvas keyword tagging + structural similarity check.
- **Example Alert:**
  > **Redundancy Echo Flagged**  
  > This mirrors logic from: `FF.100.COL – Collection Strategy`  
  > You’re trying to create a new “Bouquet Archive” but it overlaps with archived seasonal collections.  
  > *Resolution:* Merge, rename, or fork?

- **Behavior:**
  - Cross-references tag library.
  - Suggests single canonical structure unless divergence is strategic.
  - Offers immediate diff analysis on request.

---

### 3. **Logic Contradiction Alert**
- **Trigger:** Current input contradicts a previously approved decision or brand logic.
- **Detection Logic:** Semantic conflict with locked canvas content or persona rules.
- **Example Alert:**
  > **Contradiction Detected**  
  > In `FF.200.ETH`, brand tone is RH-inspired minimalism.  
  > Current request for “emoji-rich playful Hebrew” contradicts this.  
  > *Resolution:* Override tone? Fork a campaign-specific substyle?

- **Behavior:**
  - Highlights violated rule or approval.
  - Offers resolution paths: `Override`, `Temporary exception`, `Contextual fork`.

---

- **Persona Enforcement:** All alerts monitored by The Consistency Auditor and The Prompt Surgeon.
