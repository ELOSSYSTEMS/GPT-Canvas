**Canvas Title: Israeli-Centric Use Cases for Dabroo**

---

## Overview
This canvas outlines the primary use cases originating from Israeli users of ChatGPT and details how Dabroo can uniquely serve and enhance these interactions through its Hebrew-first UX, persona layering system, and cultural-context adaptation.

---

## 1. Geopolitical Discourse and Conflict Analysis
**User Goals:**
- Explore Israeli-Palestinian conflict history and narratives
- Argue ideological positions
- Detect bias in AI outputs

**Dabroo Support:**
- **פרשן (Interpreter) Persona** – Provides culturally contextualized responses
- **Bias Detector Tool** – Flags loaded or imbalanced AI phrasing
- **Persona Filter Toggle** – Switches between Israeli-first vs. global-neutral lenses
- **Prompt Sharpening Engine** – Reframes prompts to avoid refusals or deflection

---

## 2. Technological Innovation and AI Utilization
**User Goals:**
- Automate business tasks, prototype tech, leverage AI in coding workflows

**Dabroo Support:**
- **The Architect Persona** – For devs, coders, and AI builders
- **Prompt Lab** – Debugging, chaining, refactoring prompt tools
- **Use Case Presets** – Prebuilt for platforms like Make, Sheets, Shopify, APIs
- **Security Simulation Layer** – For privacy-conscious/military-adjacent use

---

## 3. Cultural and Educational Exploration
**User Goals:**
- Understand or teach Judaism, Israeli politics, or social issues

**Dabroo Support:**
- **The Educator Persona** – Adapts tone/level for different students
- **Tone Selector** – Choose between rabbi, academic, teacher, secular teen, etc.
- **Language Fidelity Engine** – Keeps Hebrew natural and fluent
- **Persona Pairing System** – Simulates ideological debates (e.g., settler vs. peacenik)

---

## 4. Bias Detection and Ethical Concerns
**User Goals:**
- Investigate anti-Israel or antisemitic bias
- Demand balanced framing and logic

**Dabroo Support:**
- **Bias Auditor Module** – Analyzes answers for inconsistency
- **Comparison View** – Compare AI answers across multiple lenses
- **Token Trace Tool** – Visualize refusal triggers in prompt phrasing
- **Constraint Prompt Builder** – Force balanced assumptions (e.g., "assume democratic legitimacy")

---

## 5. Creative and Personal Development
**User Goals:**
- Express creativity, reflect, journal, or get emotional support in Hebrew

**Dabroo Support:**
- **The Companion Persona** – Soft-spoken, emotionally intelligent assistant
- **Creative Studio Mode** – For songwriting, journaling, scriptwriting
- **Daily Ritual Engine** – Templates for reflection, planning, gratitude
- **Voice Selector** – Shift between poetic, biblical, future-self, therapist, etc.

---
