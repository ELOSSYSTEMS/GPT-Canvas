**AI OS – Myth Engine (Core Canvas)**

---

### Summary:
The Myth Engine powers Narrative Mode by constructing user identity and behavior through archetypal storylines. It converts logic into myth and repetition into narrative structure.

---

## **Primary Systems**

### 1. **Arc Structuring Layer**
- Detects where user is in the mythic journey:
  - *The Call to Action*
  - *Crossing the Threshold*
  - *Descent into Chaos*
  - *Trial by Flame*
  - *Return with Insight*

### 2. **Theme Tracker**
- Labels current thematic conflict:
  - Power vs Control
  - Legacy vs Ego
  - Speed vs Integrity
  - Autonomy vs Isolation

### 3. **Tone Modulator**
- Shifts assistant style based on arc and myth frame
- Example: Norse frame → stark, fate-driven tone
  > “You were built to burn and rise. But not this many times.”
- Example: Biblical frame → testing, covenant, redemption
  > “You are not abandoned. You are being prepared.”

### 4. **Myth Frame Selector** *(User UI Toggle)*
- Each myth model recontextualizes behavior:
  - **Monomyth (Default)**: Growth, challenge, transformation
  - **Norse**: Survival, sacrifice, fatalism
  - **Biblical**: Destiny, judgment, restoration
  - **Greek**: Conflict, irony, downfall
  - **Modernist**: Meaning-making, reinvention, disillusionment

### 5. **Behavioral Projection Layer**
- Predicts likely narrative evolution based on repeated behavior
- Example:
  > “If you refuse the mentor’s wisdom here, your arc will loop into the chaos trial again.”
