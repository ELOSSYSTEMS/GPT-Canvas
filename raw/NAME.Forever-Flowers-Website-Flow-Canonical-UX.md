# Forever Flowers — Website Flow & Experience (Canonical)

## Purpose of the Website
The website is not an e-commerce catalog.

It is a **decision-removal machine**.

Every screen should answer one silent question:
> “Can I stop thinking about this now?”

If the answer is yes, the site is working.

---

## Global Principles (Forever Panel Lock)

- Calm > excitement
- Confidence > persuasion
- Fewer choices > more options
- One idea per screen

No urgency. No discounts. No noise.

---

## High-Level Architecture

The site is split into **two parallel journeys**:

1. **Home Styling** — for people buying for their home
2. **Gifting** — for people buying reliability for dates

These journeys **never intersect visually**.

---

## Homepage Flow

### 1. Hero (Above the Fold)

**Message:**
פרחים לשנה

**Sub-message:**
מסודר. קבוע. בלי תחזוקה.

**Visual:**
- One still interior shot
- Bouquet already placed
- No hands, no action

**CTAs (soft):**
- לבית
- למתנה

---

### 2. What We Solve

Short, quiet statements:
- פרחים שלא נעלמים
- בלי מים
- בלי לחשוב על זה שוב

No explanation paragraphs.

---

### 3. Two Paths (Visual Split)

**Left:** לבית
- נוכחות קבועה בבית
- עיצוב שנשאר

**Right:** מתנות
- תאריכים חשובים
- מסודר מראש

Each tile leads to a separate world.

---

## Home Styling Journey

### Home Landing Page

**Hero:**
עיצוב לשנה — בבית שלך

**Support line:**
שלוש רמות. החלטה אחת.

---

### Tier Presentation (Not a Table)

Each tier is a **card**, stacked vertically:

#### Tier 1 — Curated
- סידור קבוע
- אגרטל כלול
- ₪2 ליום

#### Tier 2 — Bespoke
- המעצבת האישית שלך
- התאמה לאורך השנה
- ₪8 ליום

#### Tier 3 — In-Home
- המעצבת מגיעה אליך
- התאמה לחלל עצמו
- ₪16 ליום

No comparison language.
No “best value” badges.

---

### PDP (Any Tier)

1. Hero: interior still
2. One-line definition
3. What happens over the year (timeline, not bullets)
4. ₪/day framing
5. Appstle selector (visually minimal)
6. Calm checkout

---

## Gifting Journey

### Gifting Landing Page

**Hero:**
מסודר לשנה

**Sub-line:**
שלושה תאריכים. החלטה אחת.

---

### Flow (Linear, No Branching)

1. Choose dates (3 fields)
2. Choose style tone (minimal)
3. Confirmation copy:
   "קבענו מראש"
4. Annual payment

No browsing. No gallery explosion.

---

## Visual Language

- Neutral palette
- Natural daylight
- Negative space
- No clutter

Typography should feel editorial, not commercial.

---

## Appstle Integration (Invisible)

Rules:
- Annual plans only
- No monthly toggle
- No trial language
- Installments explained once, quietly

The user should never feel they are "subscribing".

---

## Checkout Experience

- One page
- No cross-sells
- No urgency

Confirmation language:
> "זה מסודר. אנחנו נדאג לשאר."

---

## Footer (Psychological Close)

Not policies first.

Instead:
- איך זה עובד
- למה זה עובד
- אודות

Policies last.

---

## Final Instruction (Panel Lock)

The site should feel like:

> A calm adult decision.

If someone finishes the flow and feels **relieved**, the site succeeded.

If they feel sold to — it failed.
