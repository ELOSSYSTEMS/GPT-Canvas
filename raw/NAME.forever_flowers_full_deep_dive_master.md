# 2. Forever Flowers Deep Dive

## 2.1 Why Does Forever Flowers Exist?
- To offer beauty that lasts beyond the fleeting life of fresh flowers.
- To fill homes, offices, hotels, and events with sustainable, low-maintenance, beautiful floral arrangements.
- To inspire timeless elegance and emotional connection.
- To reduce environmental waste and preserve meaningful decor experiences.

## 2.2 How Do We Achieve This?
- Designing and crafting premium dried and preserved flower collections.
- Providing customization and personalization options.
- Delivering warm, attentive customer service.
- Maintaining the highest standards of quality and emotional resonance.

## 2.3 What Do We Do?
- Sell dried and preserved flower bouquets.
- Supply B2B floral arrangements to businesses.
- Create bespoke floral pieces for special events.
- Offer a full online and offline customer experience.

---

## 2.4 Core Values
- Empathy towards customers.
- Legacy over short-term profit.
- Creative emotional marketing over expensive promotions.
- Momentum over perfection.
- Full transparency and trust internally and externally.

---

## 2.5 Growth Principles
- Launch and iterate: better to improve over time than wait for perfection.
- Focus on building brand equity for 5–10 years ahead.
- Outthink competitors creatively.
- Prioritize customer emotional experience.

---

## 2.6 Psychological Customer Insights
- Customers are emotionally connected to beauty and meaning.
- Perceived value is influenced by experience (unboxing, design, customization).
- Lasting beauty creates pride and connection in their homes/offices.
