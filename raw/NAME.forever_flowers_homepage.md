### Homepage Design Prompt for Forever Flowers

**Objective:**
Create an elegant, visually captivating homepage that highlights bespoke bouquets and emphasizes Forever Flowers' B2B services for Events, Restaurants, Residential Buildings, Office Flowers, and Employee Gifts. The design should feel luxurious, modern, and aligned with the premium branding of Forever Flowers.

---

### Homepage Structure

#### **Hero Section:**
- **Full-width Banner:**
  - Use a high-quality image or video showcasing a bespoke bouquet in an upscale setting (e.g., a minimalist modern home or luxurious office).
- **Text Overlay:**
  - **Headline:** "Timeless Beauty, Tailored Just for You"
  - **Subheadline:** "Preserved Floral Elegance for Homes, Businesses, and Special Occasions"
  - **CTA Buttons:**
    - "Shop Bespoke Bouquets" (links to bespoke bouquet page)
    - "Explore B2B Services" (links to B2B page)

---

#### **Featured Bouquets Section:**
- **Display a Carousel:**
  - Highlight "This Month's Forever Bouquets" with an emphasis on their limited-time availability.
  - Include **Eternal Love (אהבה נצחית)** as the featured bouquet.
  - Add a "Shop Now" button.

---

#### **Bespoke Bouquets Section:**
- **Showcase Features:**
  - Include a luxurious lifestyle image.
  - Key features to highlight:
    - Custom sizing and floral selection.
    - White-glove delivery service.
- **CTA:** "Design Your Bespoke Bouquet"

---

#### **B2B Services Section:**
- **Highlight the Five Categories:**
  - Use icons or images to represent:
    - Events
    - Restaurants
    - Residential Buildings
    - Flowers for the Office
    - Gifts for Employees
- **CTA:** "Learn More About B2B Services"

---

#### **Why Forever Flowers? Section:**
- **Grid Layout:**
  - Showcase unique selling points:
    - Long-lasting preserved flowers.
    - Eco-friendly and sustainable practices.
    - Free white-glove shipping.
    - Customizable arrangements.
- **Trust Elements:**
  - Add trust badges or a client logo carousel if applicable.

---

#### **Customer Testimonials Section:**
- **Carousel-Style Testimonials:**
  - Feature reviews and quotes from satisfied customers or businesses.

---

#### **Footer CTA:**
- **Newsletter Sign-Up Form:**
  - "Stay Updated on New Bouquets and Special Offers"
- **Links:**
  - Add links to social media platforms and contact details.

---

### Styling Guidelines
- **Typography:**
  - Use elegant serif fonts
