**AI OS – Onboarding Logic & User Tier Trees**

**Purpose:** Define the onboarding flow for three user tiers (Beginner, Moderate, Power User). This framework ensures the system adapts instantly to a user's familiarity level, reducing friction while unlocking depth over time.

---

**I. Entry Prompt: Choose Your Experience**

Upon installation or first use, AI OS presents the user with:

> “Welcome to AI OS – your personal assistant operating system.”

> “Before we begin, how do you prefer to work with AI?”

### **Tier Options:**

1. **Beginner**
   > “I want to feel supported. I don’t know much about prompts or personas, but I want help thinking clearly, staying focused, and getting things done.”

2. **Moderate**
   > “I use ChatGPT regularly. I’ve tried prompt tools and extensions. I want more control, but not complexity. Show me how to think better with AI.”

3. **Power User**
   > “I know exactly what I want. I’ve built systems before. Give me the modes, modules, memory, and full OS-level control.”

---

**II. Onboarding Tree: Beginner Path**

- **Auto-activate:**
  - Motherly Mode
  - Teacher Persona
  - Daily Planner Lite
  - Journal tool (simplified UI)

- **Walkthrough Includes:**
  - “Click here to write what’s on your mind”
  - “Let’s create a simple plan for today”
  - “This is your assistant’s current tone: calm and supportive”
  - Tooltip on canvas system: “Think of this as your assistant’s memory”

- **Feature Locks/Guidance:**
  - Advanced persona builder hidden by default
  - Complex canvas management disabled
  - Encouraged to explore more features after 3 days of use

---

**III. Onboarding Tree: Moderate Path**

- **Auto-activate:**
  - Sharp Mode or default toggle chooser
  - Planner + Journal both enabled
  - 3 core personas preloaded (Teacher, Strategist, Operator)

- **Walkthrough Includes:**
  - “Want to shift tone? Click Modes”
  - “Personas change how your assistant thinks. Try one.”
  - “You can store thoughts, plans, and strategies here” (canvas system)

- **Guidance vs Friction Balance:**
  - Tooltips for all buttons
  - Mini-tutorials unlock as user explores (e.g. “Try your first journal entry”)

---

**IV. Onboarding Tree: Power User Path**

- **Auto-activate:**
  - Sharp Mode + Prompt Surgeon + Consistency Auditor
  - Full access to mode/logic/persona/canvas modules
  - Blank workspace ready to build own OS stack

- **Walkthrough Includes (minimal):**
  - 1-line prompts: “Create your first persona” / “Toggle your preferred modules” / “Import your own canvas”
  - No tooltips unless requested
  - Option to explore beginner/moderate overlay from settings

---

**V. Optional Path Switching**
- All tiers include: “Switch experience type” button in settings
- After 7 days, prompt user: “Want to try Power Mode?”
