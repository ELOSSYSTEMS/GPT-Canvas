# FOREVER FLOWERS – STRATEGY & VISION

**Purpose**: Define the long-term strategic direction of Forever Flowers including positioning, market expansion, pricing evolution, and internal transformation roadmap.

---

## CORE POSITIONING
- **Category**: Premium dried & preserved floral design
- **Model**: Boutique, limited drops, high-touch service
- **Differentiators**:
  - Not boxed. Hand delivered.
  - Real florists, real art—not factory-built kitsch
  - Seasonal collections + Atelier bespoke service

---

## LONG-TERM OBJECTIVES
- Dominate the preserved floral space in Israel
- Grow B2B base with luxury hotel/hospitality clients
- Expand Atelier as flagship tier and thought-leader showcase
- Build subscription model with curated lifestyle delivery
- Solidify brand as synonymous with elevated Israeli gifting

---

## STRATEGIC PRIORITIES
1. **Operational Optimization**:  
   Automate logistics, client tracking, and production timelines (Make + Google Sheets)

2. **Sales Infrastructure**:  
   Dedicated pipeline system for B2B with customized proposals, follow-ups, and pricing models

3. **Visual Identity**:  
   Harmonize wrapping, cards, and packaging to evoke brand values (olive green, bone, minimalist elegance)

4. **Online Experience**:  
   Optimize Shopify flow, collection architecture, and CRO tools

5. **Financial Clarity**:  
   Forecasting for seasonality, big accounts (e.g., Arcaffe, Fattal), and personal + business cashflow
