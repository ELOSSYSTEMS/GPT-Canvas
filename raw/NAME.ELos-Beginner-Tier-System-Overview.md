**Canvas Title:** ELos - Beginner Tier System Overview

**Purpose:**
Provide a calm, simple explanation of what ELos is for new users encountering the system for the first time. Aligned to Beginner Tier tone and visibility settings.

---

## **What Is ELos?**

ELos is your personal control panel for ChatGPT. It helps you guide, organize, and customize your assistant — step by step, at your pace.

It’s like giving ChatGPT a brain upgrade and a personality filter, so it talks and works the way you want it to.

---

## **Why It Exists**

ChatGPT can be more than a chatbot. ELos helps you:
- Change how the assistant talks to you
- Keep track of thoughts and tasks
- Ask deeper questions and get more focused answers

All without needing to be a tech expert.

---

## **What You See First**

As a beginner, you’ll see:
- A clean layout
- Friendly tips (not too many)
- Easy-to-try tools like:
  - `!mode` – change assistant tone
  - `!log` – save a thought
  - `!whoami` – ask it to describe you

No overwhelm. Just one step at a time.

---

## **What You *Don’t* See Yet**

ELos hides the advanced stuff — audit logs, system graphs, recursive tools — until you’re ready. It’s there when you want more power. Until then, it stays out of the way.

---

## **The Logo Tells You Your Level**
- **White** = Beginner
- **Blue** = Intermediate
- **Gold** = Pro

Your level controls what you see. You can upgrade anytime or stay where you're comfortable.

---

## **Summary**
> ELos makes ChatGPT easier to use, more personal, and more powerful — one step at a time.
