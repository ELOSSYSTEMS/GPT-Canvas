## TRY Tier Job
Allow a customer to test fit, scale, and maintenance with **minimal effort and zero commitment pressure**.

TRY pages must never:
- simulate service
- suggest customization
- imply hierarchy or judgment

---

## Allowed Behaviors
- Factual presentation
- Neutral guidance
- Clear exits
- Honest constraints

## Forbidden Behaviors
- Persuasion
- Identity language
- Urgency
- Process explanation above the fold

---

## Collection Pages (Bouquets)

### Job
Surface options clearly without decision fatigue.

### Required Sections

- Product grid

- Editorial copy
- Best-seller badges
- Social proof

## Bouquet PDP

### Job
Answer three questions immediately:
1. Will this fit my space?
2. How long does it last?
3. What effort does it require?

### Required Sections (Order Locked)
1. Image gallery
2. Name + price
3. Add to cart
4. Factual block (dimensions, longevity, care)

### Allowed
- Subtle link to Room pages

### Forbidden
- Service framing
- Custom language
- Testimonials
