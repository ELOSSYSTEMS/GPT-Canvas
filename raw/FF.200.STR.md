**Canvas: FF.200.STR – Brand Story & Narrative**

---

**📍 Purpose:**
Define the emotional narrative and foundational brand story that moves Forever Flowers from a gifting brand to an interior design studio. This canvas breaks the mental association of "flowers as a gesture" and rebuilds it as "flowers as identity and design logic."

---

**🧭 Strategic Shift:**
- **Old Frame:** Flowers are ephemeral gifts — bought occasionally for someone else.
- **New Frame:** Flowers are permanent design objects — purchased intentionally to transform the environment we inhabit.

---

**🧠 Core Narrative Pillars:**

1. **Presence, Not Performance:**
   > “Our flowers aren’t for celebration — they’re for stillness.”
   - Move away from moment-driven storytelling.
   - Anchor in long-term aesthetic value and internal emotional resonance.

2. **Design as Ritual:**
   > “Floral arrangements aren’t accessories. They’re anchors.”
   - Every Forever Flowers piece is part of a larger intentional living system.
   - Interior styling becomes a self-care act — not decoration, but calibration.

3. **Objects with Memory:**
   > “Not fresh. Not fading. Not replaceable.”
   - The permanence of preserved flowers reframes value around durability.
   - Opposes disposable culture.

4. **The Home as Gallery:**
   > “We don’t sell flowers. We sell form.”
   - Every bouquet is part of a composition.
   - Naming conventions, layout logic, and collection structure reference art curation.

---

**🗣️ Tone & Language:**
- Neutral, confident, spare.
- Fewer words, more weight.
- Rooted in **Restoration Hardware** and **Habitat** tone structure — heavy whitespace, poetic restraint.
- No emojis, no over-explanation, no urgency.

---

**🎨 Examples of Story Framing in Copy:**
- "Form, not flora."
- "A bouquet that doesn't beg for attention — it holds it."
- "Designed to stay. Made to shift the room."
- "Not sent. Placed."
