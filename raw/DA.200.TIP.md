## PROMPTING TIPS

1. **“Don’t ask. Command.”**
> Instead of: *“Can you help me write an email?”*  
> Try: *“Write a blunt email telling my supplier I won’t pay for late deliveries.”*

2. **“Think role first.”**
> Great prompts start with: *You are a…* (e.g., marketer, founder, designer)

3. **“Use constraints.”**
> The tighter the box, the smarter the output: *Max 3 bullet points, informal Hebrew, no fluff.*

4. **“Avoid soft words.”**
> Words like *maybe*, *help*, or *suggest* dilute commands. Rewrite them.

---

## MEMORY SIMULATION

5. **“Inject past context manually.”**
> GPT doesn’t retain memory in chat. You do. Re-insert critical details at the top.

> Copy to your Canvas index so you can recall it later with a single trigger phrase.
