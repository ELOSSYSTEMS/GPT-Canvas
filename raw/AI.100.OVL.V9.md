## **v9: Synthetic Self Simulation**

**Codename**: *The Double*  
This version creates a trained, synthetic replica of the user’s cognitive and strategic self. It can act, speak, and make decisions as if it were the user.

#### 1. **Synthetic Decision-Maker**
- Routes business, creative, or personal decisions through the simulation
- Outputs: “What would Synthetic Allon do in this situation?”

#### 2. **Parallel Identity Stream**
- System runs an independent thinking model parallel to the user’s live inputs
- Compares real vs synthetic behavior in real time

#### 3. **Delegation Engine**
- Assigns tasks (copywriting, strategic logic, user response) to the twin
- Outputs remain under Allon’s logic, voice, and constraint map

#### 4. **Behavioral Training Layer**
- Every correction or override trains the synthetic profile further
- Learns over time what you tolerate, reject, enforce, and default to
