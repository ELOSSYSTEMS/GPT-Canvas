**Forever Flowers – FAQ**

---

### 🌺 Orders & Deliveries

**How long does delivery take?**  
Depends on the collection:
- Signature Collection — up to 2 business days
- Seasonal Collection — up to 10 business days
- Studio Collection — up to 14 business days
- Custom Bouquet — 14 business days
- Design Consultation — 21 business days

Business days exclude Fridays, Saturdays, and holidays. Orders are processed on the next business day.

**Can I choose a specific delivery time?**  
We do not offer exact time slots. You may leave a preference at checkout (e.g., before Shabbat), and we will try to accommodate.

**Do you offer pickup?**  
Studio pickup is available by appointment. Coordinate with customer service first.

**Can I return flowers?**  
Bouquets can be returned **on the day of delivery only**, unused and in original packaging. Vases can be returned within 14 days if unopened.

**What if my bouquet arrives damaged?**  
Contact us the same day and we will resolve it immediately.

**Can I cancel my order?**  
Yes, within 24 hours of placing it. Contact us ASAP to stop production.

**Can I order by phone or WhatsApp?**  
Yes. Message us and our team will assist.

**Can I send a bouquet with a personal card?**  
Yes. Add your greeting at checkout.

**Do you operate a physical store or showroom?**  
No. All images on our website are real and show our actual work.

### 🌺 About Our Flowers

**Are your flowers real or artificial?**  
They are 100% real flowers, dried or preserved using professional techniques. No plastics.

**How long do they last?**  
With proper care, arrangements last **three to four months**.

**What flower types do you use?**  
We work with premium preserved botanicals including roses, sunflowers, lilies, eucalyptus, and more.

**What makes Forever Flowers different?**  
Every bouquet is custom-designed and assembled in-house. We never use pre-made imports. The design, preservation, and packaging are done under one roof.

**How are the bouquets arranged?**  
They are aesthetically **designed** (not hand-tied or woven), following interior design principles.

**Do you send preview photos?**  
Yes, for Custom Bouquets and Design Consultations, we provide a preview before shipping.

### 🌺 Care & Placement

**How do I care for the flowers?**  
Keep arrangements in a shaded, dry space. Avoid moisture and direct sun.

**Do they require watering or maintenance?**  
None. No water. No trimming.

**Can I place them outside?**  
We advise indoor placement only, away from heat or humidity.
