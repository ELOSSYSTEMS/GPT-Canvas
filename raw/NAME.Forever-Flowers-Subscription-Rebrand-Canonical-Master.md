# Forever Flowers — Subscription Rebrand

## Canonical Master Document
**Status:** Canonical
**Purpose:** Single source of truth for concept, site structure, and marketing of the Forever Flowers subscription-led brand.

---

## 1. Core Concept (Forever Panel Consensus)

### What We Are Really Selling
Not flowers.

We sell **removal of decay, decisions, and forgetting**.

Forever Flowers is a *domestic design system* and a *relationship reliability system* — delivered through flowers.

---

### Reframing Rule (Hard Lock)

- Never sell bouquets
- Never sell frequency
- Never sell quantity

**Always sell time horizon + relief**

Examples:
- "פרחים לשנה"
- "מסודר לשנה"
- "לא צריך לחשוב על זה יותר"

---

### Forever Panel Alignment

- **Tamar Kay:** Israelis buy calm, not luxury. Predictability beats excitement.
- **Rory Sutherland:** This is loss-aversion engineering (preventing panic and decay).
- **Steve Jobs:** One idea, three expressions. No feature storytelling.
- **Alex Hormozi:** ₪/day framing converts luxury into prudence.
- **Seth Godin:** Subscribers are a tribe — people who solved flowers.

---

## 2. Subscription Architecture (Mental Model)

### Two Parallel Systems (Do Not Merge)

#### A. Home Subscription (Design Ownership)
For people who want their home to feel designed — all year.

Includes:
- Vase ownership
- Style continuity
- Long-term presence

#### B. Gifting Subscription (Reliability Engine)
For people who want important dates handled — forever.

Includes:
- No vase
- Fixed dates
- Zero involvement after setup

> These systems must never be compared side-by-side.

---

## 3. Subscription Tiers (Internal Definition)

### Home Subscriptions

**Tier 1 — Curated Style**
- Entry-level design ownership
- Vase included
- ₪2/day framing

**Tier 2 — Bespoke (Remote)**
- Personal consultation with Shiran
- ₪8/day framing

**Tier 3 — In-Home Concierge**
- Shiran visits the home
- ₪16/day framing

### Gifting Subscription

**Gifting — Set & Forget**
- Cheapest tier
- Bouquet-only (no vase)
- 3 pre-defined dates
- Automatic delivery

## 4. Website Architecture (Shopify + Appstle)

### Global Rule
The word **"subscription"** should appear as little as possible.

---

### Primary Navigation

- Home
- לבית (Home Styling)
- מתנות (Gifting)
- איך זה עובד
- אודות

---

### Appstle Implementation (Canonical)

#### PDP Structure (Home Subscriptions)

1. Hero: "פרחים לשנה"
2. Tier selector (visual, not tabular)
3. ₪/day framing
4. Appstle plan selector (hidden mechanics)
5. Installments note (calm, not promotional)
6. Checkout


#### Gifting Flow (Dedicated Page)

1. Headline: "לא שוכחים יותר"
2. Three-date selector (Anniversary / Birthday / Valentine’s as defaults)
3. Style selector (minimal)
4. Appstle annual plan
5. Silent confirmation copy

> No bouquet images explosion. Calm confidence only.

---

### Critical UX Rules

- No comparison tables between gifting and home
- No urgency timers
- No discount language
- No bouquet counts above the fold

---

## 5. Marketing Strategy (Forever Panel Approved)

### Core Message Pillars

1. **Time** — handled for a year
2. **Calm** — no decay, no forgetting
3. **Design** — belongs in the home
4. **Reliability** — shows up when it matters

---

### Home Subscription Marketing

Target:
- Women 30–55
- Design-oriented households

Messaging:
- "נוכחות קבועה"
- "עיצוב שנשאר"
- "לא טרנד, לא עונתי"

Creative:
- Stillness
- Negative space
- Interior context

---

### Gifting Marketing

Target:
- Men
- Partners
- Busy professionals

Messaging:
- "קבענו מראש"
- "מסודר לשנה"
- "בלי תזכורות"

Creative:
- No romance clichés
- No hearts
- No couples imagery

---

## 6. Language & Tone Rules (Non-Negotiable)

- Calm authority
- Matter-of-fact confidence

- Explanations
- Overchoice
