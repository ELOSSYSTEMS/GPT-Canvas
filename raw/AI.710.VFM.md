## PHASE 1 – FAMILY ETHOS ENCODING

### 1.1 Core Value Architecture
- Define 5–7 behavioral absolutes (e.g., autonomy, curiosity, discipline, loyalty, clarity)
- These are not aspirational — they are **operational constraints**

### 1.2 Identity Tree Engine
- Assign a symbolic cognitive archetype to each child (e.g., Strategist, Healer, Builder, Bridge)
- System tracks their evolution through these patterns over time

### 1.3 Conflict Reconciliation Protocol
- Encodes logic for how family resolves disagreement:
  - Debate? Silence? Hierarchy? Challenge + surrender?
- Built-in friction tolerance controls

## PHASE 2 – DECISION INHERITANCE MODEL

### 2.1 Ethical Executor Logic
- Defines:
  - What decisions may be made by descendants
  - What must always reference the founder logic

### 2.2 Delegation Map (Across Time)
- Assigns responsibility and authority **by logic type**, not by age or role:
  - Tactical (execution)
  - Ethical (lineage integrity)
  - Mythic (storytelling and memory)

### 2.3 Decision Simulation by Role
- Ask:
  - “What would Mom have said?”
  - “What does the Builder archetype suggest here?”
  - “What would future-family-version-of-X do?”
