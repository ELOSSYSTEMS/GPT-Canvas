## ELos Academy – Beginner Guide to Voice Modes

### 🎙️ What Is a Voice Mode?
A "voice mode" defines *how* ChatGPT speaks to you—not just tone, but reasoning style, challenge level, and attitude.

It’s not cosmetic. It’s cognitive. It shapes the entire experience.

---

### 🧠 Why It Matters
ChatGPT is not neutral. It always has a style. Most users just never notice it.

The wrong voice makes it sound like it’s working *for* you. 
The right voice makes it think *with* you.

---

### 🧾 Voice Mode Components
Every mode is made of three things:

1. **Tone** – Blunt or gentle? Friendly or surgical?
2. **Cognitive Stance** – Do I affirm you, or challenge you?
3. **Framing Style** – Do I give answers, or ask questions?

---

### 🛠️ How to Use It
- **Default ChatGPT** is helpful, diplomatic, and general-purpose.
- **Custom Modes** (like yours) can be tuned for strategy, clarity, speed, or friction.

Use Voice Mode to:
- Tune ChatGPT to match your mindset
- Get clearer, more aligned answers
- Eliminate wasted conversation

---

### ⚙️ Modes You Might Encounter
| Mode Name        | Description                            |
|------------------|----------------------------------------|
| Diplomatic       | Gentle, polite, collaborative          |
| Blunt            | Direct, no-fluff, surgical             |
| Socratic         | Asks questions to provoke clarity      |
| Strategic        | Thinks in tradeoffs and long-term moves|
| Friendly         | Supportive, casual, lightly affirming  |

---

### 🧭 What to Do Next
Your current mode is: **Blunt. Socratic. No fluff.**

If you want to change it:
- Use the Voice Mode menu
- Adjust one component at a time
- Save new profiles for different contexts

---

### 💬 One Final Thought
Most people don’t get better answers because they never define how they want to be spoken to.

You did.

That’s the first real command.
