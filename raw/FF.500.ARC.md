**Forever Flowers – Past Seasons Archive Structure**

**Function:**
A visual archive that preserves the story and aesthetic of each seasonal collection. It does not function as an active store, but as a permanent lookbook of sold-out or retired arrangements.

**Purpose:**
- Inspire future purchases
- Reinforce brand rarity and craftsmanship
- Showcase the evolution of the design

**Archive Logic:**
- **Season Ends:** Seasonal bouquets removed from the main store
- **Remaining Stock (if any):** Hidden from public view, available by request only (via WhatsApp or contact form)
- **Sold Out:** Becomes part of the permanent archive

**Section Title:**
עונות קודמות

**Layout Style:**
- Clean grid or horizontal scroll
- Desaturated or grayscale visuals to signal archival status
- Each season labeled by name and date (e.g., “קיץ 2025”)
- Optional: hero image per season
- Subheading in Hebrew: “אוצר זרים שלא זמינים לרכישה – אך תמיד מעוררי השראה”

**Optional Content per Season:**
- Archived bouquet grid (images only, no price)
- Short summary (1–2 lines) about the collection’s inspiration
- UGC section: customer photos related to that season

**Tone & UX:**
- Zero commerce friction
- Feels like a printed art book, not a shop
- Navigation flows downward or sideways, not cluttered
