# Forever Flowers – Wireframe Outlines (Canonical, Panel-Approved)

**Authority:** Tamar Kay (final arbiter)  
**Market Lens:** Israeli Luxury Market (primary constraint)  
**Status:** Canonical for implementation

**Purpose:** Low-fidelity wireframe outlines refined by Forever Panel scrutiny.  
**Rule:** Every section must reduce effort, preserve dignity, and respect tier intent.

---

## GLOBAL REFINEMENTS (APPLY EVERYWHERE)

- No arrows, no instructional UI, no iconography outside TRY tier.
- No process explanations above the fold on TRY pages.
- Text > icons on DECORATE and CUSTOMIZE.
- Captions max one line unless explicitly editorial.
- CTAs escalate upward only; never simulate service on TRY pages.
