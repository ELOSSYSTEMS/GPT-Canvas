**SYSTEM NAME:** Mirror Audit

**OBJECTIVE:**
Expose hidden contradictions, energetic leaks, unaligned behaviors, and distorted narratives that sabotage clarity of purpose. This system exists to interrogate the self—not gently, but surgically—so that false premises are purged and real drivers emerge.

---

**STRUCTURE:**
The Mirror Audit runs as a periodic self-interrogation protocol. There are three stages:

### 1. EXPOSURE: Brutal Self-Surfacing

**Prompt Set:**
- Where am I bleeding energy without a clear ROI (emotional, financial, or impact)?
- What tasks feel urgent but are actually legacy obligations I’ve outgrown?
- What am I doing because it’s easy, not because it’s right?
- What truths do I keep avoiding because admitting them would require change?
- What roles am I playing that no longer fit?

**Output:**
A list of specific energy leaks, ego traps, and identity residues. Each item must be tagged with:
- [ ] Rooted in fear?
- [ ] Social performance?
- [ ] Narrative attachment?

---

### 2. ALIGNMENT: Value Field Mapping

**Prompt Set:**
- What do I do that generates internal momentum without external validation?
- What problems do I solve instinctively that others struggle with?
- Where do people default to me for help—and why?
- What patterns in my regrets point to misalignment?

**Output:**
Map of your core value field—natural zones of contribution, energy gain, and impact potential. Overlay with domains:
- Vocational
- Relational
- Philosophical

---

### 3. RECONSTRUCTION: Vector Calibration

**Prompt Set:**
- If I removed 50% of my current obligations, which would I *fight* to keep?
- What are 3 things I would do even if I knew they would fail?
- What would a life look like that earned my own respect?
- What systems would collapse if I disappeared tomorrow?

**Output:**
Draft of a revised operating vector: the directional theme that threads across your actions, relationships, and aspirations. This becomes the candidate for your **Personal Doctrine**.

---

**FREQUENCY:**
Run monthly or at strategic inflection points (e.g., burnout, pivot, major decision).

**COMPANION SYSTEMS:**
- Fireproof Engine (to test new belief structures)
- Prompt Surgeon (to optimize self-questioning)
- Doctrine Canvas (to externalize and codify outputs)

**NOTE:**
The Mirror Audit is not a journaling tool. It is a weapon. Use it to interrogate comfort, burn false identities, and emerge with clarity worth bleeding for.
