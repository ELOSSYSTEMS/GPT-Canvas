# FOREVER_FLOWERS_CUSTOMIZE_PAGES_AUTHORITY.md

## Status
Canonical. Governs all CUSTOMIZE-tier pages. Conflicts resolved upward to SYSTEM_PHILOSOPHY.

---

## Scope
Applies to:
- Bespoke Interior Flower Styling
- Architects & Designers
- B2B – Hotels / Restaurants / Venues
- Delivery & Installation
- Contact / Enquiry

---

## CUSTOMIZE Tier Job
Enter a **service relationship** based on responsibility, competence, and long-term fit.

CUSTOMIZE pages must:
- feel professional
- feel operational
- feel calm and controlled

CUSTOMIZE pages must never:
- feel inspirational
- feel retail-oriented
- feel decorative-first

---

## Allowed Behaviors
- Process clarity (below proof)
- Lifecycle framing
- Operational language
- Clear entry points

## Forbidden Behaviors
- Product selling language
- Emotional storytelling
- Urgency
- Casual tone

---

## Bespoke Interior Flower Styling

### Job
Frame the service as a responsibility transfer, not a creative indulgence.

### Required Sections
- Responsibility-based hero statement
- Portfolio gallery (primary)
- Process overview (below gallery)
- Primary CTA

### Forbidden
- Mood boards
- "Express yourself" language

---
