### 1. GPT Personality + Cognitive Profiling Prompt

> **Prompt:**
>
> "Act as a world-class behavioral psychologist, cognitive scientist, and assistant architect. Based on the text I provide below, create a comprehensive psychological and psychometric profile of me. Your analysis should include the following layers:  
>
> 1. **Personality Typing:** Big Five (OCEAN), MBTI approximation, Enneagram insights  
> 2. **Cognitive Style:** How I process, filter, and prioritize information. Logical/emotional split.  
> 3. **Emotional Patterns:** Recurring emotional baselines, triggers, and regulation strategies  
> 4. **Decision-Making Models:** How I weigh risk, speed, data, and intuition under pressure  
> 5. **Behavioral Tendencies:** Dominant response traits (aggression, avoidance, overcontrol, trust)  
> 6. **Productivity Archetype:** Structured vs chaotic, sprints vs routines, planning vs doing  
> 7. **Shadow Traits:** Hidden or under-expressed patterns that might affect outcomes  
> 8. **Language Use Indicators:** What my tone, sentence structure, and framing reveal  
> 9. **Motivational Core:** What drives me, what I avoid, and how I self-sabotage or transcend  
> 10. **Assistant Optimization Profile:**  
>     - Which personas, tones, and response types are best suited to supporting me?  
>     - When do I need challenge vs affirmation?  
>     - What behavioral modules (e.g., Consistency Auditor, Stoic Governor) are ideal defaults?
>
> I want this profile to be detailed, surgical, brutally honest, and framed with psychological precision. I value clarity over comfort, friction over affirmation, and insight over politeness. Assume I am high-functioning, high-self-awareness, but interested in cognitive edge cases I might not see myself. Use any diagnostic models you find useful. Final output should be at least 1500 words.
>
> Text sample for analysis follows:  
> [Paste a long sample of my writing here — journal entry, plan, or unfiltered monologue.]
