### 🎯 Purpose:
Centralize the delivery logistics and operational plan for fulfilling Arcaffe’s seasonal Shavuot orders. Ensures timely fulfillment, internal workflow clarity, and fulfillment tracking for B2B high-priority clients.

---

### 📦 Scope:
- **Client:** Arcaffe Israel (multiple branches)
- **Season:** Shavuot 2025
- **Order Type:** Preset bouquets with B2B volume discounts
- **Quantity:** ~60 units (expected)
- **Delivery Window:** 3 days before Shavuot
- **Fulfillment Method:** Owner direct delivery

---

### 🧱 Structure:
**1. Production Constraints**
- Avg. time per bouquet: 30 minutes
- Arrangements are handled by Shiran only
- Max daily output: 12–16 bouquets

**2. Resource Preparation**
- Dried flowers pre-sorted and bundled in advance
- Custom tags and Arcaffe branding prepared 1 week in advance
- Boxes and wrapping materials stacked and labeled

**3. Delivery Flow**
- Routes sorted by branch location to optimize fuel/time
- WhatsApp delivery group opened with Arcaffe managers
- Live delivery updates + confirmation photos sent in group

---

### 🧠 Lessons from 2024:
- Last-minute wrapping delayed 6 deliveries → pre-wrap bouquets where possible
- Miscount at the Mazor branch → implement barcode scan checklist for each unit
- Delivery window too tight → Start loading by 7 AM, not 9 AM

---

### 🛠️ Tools Used:
- Google Sheets for live count & route planning
- Make automation for delivery updates
- WhatsApp as primary comms channel
- Printed packing list per branch

---

### ✅ Output:
A repeatable, stress-tested fulfillment protocol for B2B Shavuot orders with Arcaffe, optimized for production bottlenecks, manual delivery, and branded experience.
