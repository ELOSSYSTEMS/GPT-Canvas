### 🧠 Purpose
To define the official structure, naming conventions, and logic governing all Forever Flowers collections. This canvas ensures every bouquet belongs to a coherent ecosystem that aligns with visual tone, pricing tier, and customer expectations.

---

### 📚 Collection Architecture

#### 1. **Signature Collection**  
- **Definition:** Evergreen, timeless designs optimized for everyday use and interior decor
- **Aesthetic:** Minimalist, warm neutrals, clean lines  
- **Materials:** Primarily dried flowers, high visual longevity
- **Usage Context:** Housewarming, self-purchase, casual gifting
- **Tone:** Elegant simplicity
- **Price Range:** ₪190–₪390
- **Delivery:** 1–2 business days

#### 2. **Seasonal Collection**  
- **Definition:** Limited edition bouquets tied to Israeli holidays and natural seasonal shifts
- **Aesthetic:** Textural and color shifts based on the season (e.g., brighter tones in spring, muted tones in winter)
- **Examples:** Shavuot, Rosh Hashanah, Sukkot
- **Materials:** Mix of dried + preserved as needed
- **Tone:** Celebratory, warm, timely
- **Price Range:** ₪250–₪470
- **Delivery:** Pre-order or timed campaign windows

#### 3. **Atelier Collection**  
- **Definition:** Handcrafted artistic bouquets—each with a name, narrative, and distinct styling
- **Aesthetic:** Experimental, poetic, includes rare preserved elements or designer vessels
- **Materials:** High-end preserved florals, custom packaging
- **Tone:** Artistic, rare, statement pieces
- **Price Range:** ₪450–₪890+
- **Delivery:** 7–10 business days, made-to-order

---

### 🔠 Naming Conventions
- **Signature:** Names based on simplicity, emotions, or colors (e.g., "Calm", "Ivory", "Wheat")
- **Seasonal:** Names tied to holiday themes or seasonal cues (e.g., "Bloom for Shavuot", "First Rain")
- **Atelier:** Names with storytelling weight (e.g., "Stillness", "Echo", "Letters from the North")

---

### 🔁 Cross-Collection Guidelines
- No duplication of names between collections
- Each bouquet must pass through visual QA for consistency within its collection
- Seasonal collections expire and archive—can be referenced in the Lookbook
- Atelier items receive a mini product essay for site + social
