**Canvas Title: New Approach to Dabroo AI Freemium Plus**

---

## 1. Core Vision
- Transform ChatGPT into a distinctly Israeli-first, hyper-personalized AI interface.
- Hook users via a freemium extension loaded with visual and UX customizations; land, then expand into full-featured API-driven Pro plan.

---

## 2. Freemium Layer (No API Calls)
### Layout & Localization
- **RTL Layout**: Flip entire UI to right-to-left by default.
- **Hebrew-First Interface**: Translate buttons, menus, labels into natural Hebrew.

### Personalization Suite
- **Custom Themes**: Background color picker, font chooser, theme presets.
- **Logo & Branding**: Replace sidebar title with "Dabroo AI" and custom icon.
- **Preset Personas**: Offer 20+ built-in personas (e.g., "The Translator", "The Teacher", "The Analyst"); toggle via UI.

### Onboarding & Guidance
- **Guided Tour**: Tooltip-driven walkthrough on first use (Intro.js/Shepherd.js).
- **In-App Tips**: Contextual hints for hidden features and Pro previews.
- **Documentation**: Quick-start cheat sheets in Hebrew for common tasks.

---

## 3. Pro Tier Previews & Sneak Peeks
- **Locked Personas**: Show greyed-out advanced personas with preview tags.
- **Prompt Allowance**: Tease 10 free persona-powered prompts as a trial.
- **Feature Callouts**: Inline prompts: “Unlock Bias Auditor in Pro” etc.

---

## 4. Conversion Strategy
1. **Freemium Hook**: Deep personalization drives daily use.
2. **Curiosity Triggers**: Highlight locked features and usage counters.
3. **Upgrade Nudges**: Soft pop-ups after 20 free prompts or upon accessing locked persona.
4. **Pricing Messaging**: Clear value: "3× Personas + Unlimited Customization".
5. **Payment Flow**: Seamless in-extension purchase via Stripe/Lemon Squeezy.

---

## 5. Israeli-Centric Enhancements
- **Cultural Snippets**: Local idioms, references, holiday modes.
- **Local Data Feeds**: Israeli news headlines, calendar events, weather.
- **Right Sidebar Integration**: Native-feel repositioned controls.

---

## 6. Support & Resources
- **Help Center**: Hebrew-language KB articles and video guides.
- **Community Forum**: Israeli user community for feedback and feature requests.
- **Changelog Overlay**: Pop-up on updates highlighting new local features.
