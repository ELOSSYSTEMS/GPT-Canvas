### Forever Flowers by Schaham Home: Comprehensive Rebrand Strategy

#### **Brand Essence and Identity**
- **New Mission Statement**: "Redefining timeless beauty with sustainable floral art."
- **Brand Values**:
  1. Sustainability: Eco-friendly products that last many months without maintenance.
  2. Elegance: Sophisticated designs for discerning customers.
  3. Customization: Unique and personal floral experiences for every client.

#### **Visual Identity**
- **Logo**: Simplified, minimalist logo featuring a blooming flower within an infinity symbol to represent timelessness.
- **Color Palette**: Focus on muted luxury tones such as cream, soft gold, deep green, and blush pink.
- **Typography**: Elegant serif fonts for titles (e.g., Playfair Display) paired with clean sans-serif fonts for body text (e.g., Lato).
- **Packaging**: Use premium recycled materials. Incorporate embossed or foil-stamped logos on neutral-toned boxes and ribbons.

#### **Product Offerings**
1. **New Collection Names and Concepts**:
   - **Eternal Elegance**: High-end luxury bouquets, ideal for premium customers.
   - **Rustic Bliss**: Earthy, nature-inspired arrangements with palm leaves and acorns.
   - **Modern Minimalist**: Sleek, compact designs for table decor and offices.
   - **Seasonal Stunners**: Bouquets inspired by seasonal themes and colors.

2. **Customization Options**:
   - Bespoke bouquets with tiered pricing.
   - Upsell options: vases, fuller arrangements, photo approval, and personalized messages.

3. **Subscription Service**:
   - "Eternal Blooms Club": Monthly delivery of curated bouquets with a loyalty rewards program.

#### **Target Audience**
- Refined focus on luxury home decor enthusiasts, boutique hotels, and corporate clients.
- Demographics: 30-65+, predominantly female, with an interest in sustainability and design.

#### **Website Redesign**
- **Theme**: Ultra-luxurious and user-friendly (e.g., Shopify Prestige theme).
- **Hero Banner**: Video showcasing bouquet creation with soft instrumental music.
- **Navigation**: Simplify categories to: Collections, Custom Bouquets, Subscriptions, and Corporate Solutions.
- **B2B Landing Page**: Highlight benefits for hotels and offices.

#### **Marketing Strategy**
1. **Social Media**:
   - Visual storytelling on Instagram and Pinterest.
   - Collaborations with influencers in luxury and eco-friendly niches.

2. **Paid Ads**:
   - Google Shopping campaigns for specific collections.
   - Meta ads targeting luxury buyers and corporate clients.

3. **Content Creation**:
   - Blog topics: Floral decor tips, sustainability in design, and hotel collaborations.
   - Videos: Behind-the-scenes bouquet creation, customer testimonials, and DIY tutorials.

4. **PR Campaign**:
   - Reach out to lifestyle magazines and bloggers for features.
   - Sponsorships for boutique events and eco-friendly exhibitions.

#### **Operational Improvements**
1. **Streamlined Logistics**:
   - Regional delivery zones with scheduled delivery days.
   - Partner with third-party logistics companies for cost-efficiency.

2. **Customer Experience**:
   - White glove delivery service.
   - Follow-up surveys and loyalty rewards for feedback.

3. **Sustainability Practices**:
   - Offset carbon emissions for deliveries.
   - Educate customers on the eco-friendliness of preserved flowers.

#### **Timeline for Rebrand**
1. **Phase 1 (Month 1-2)**: Finalize new visual identity, product concepts, and website mockups.
2. **Phase 2 (Month 3-4)**: Launch website, new collections, and social media campaigns.
3. **Phase 3 (Month 5)**: Implement subscription service and evaluate initial feedback.
4. **Phase 4 (Ongoing)**: Monitor performance, refine strategies, and expand B2B partnerships.

#### **Goals for Rebrand**
1. Increase monthly revenue to 100,000 NIS within 6 months.
2. Secure contracts with 5 additional boutique hotels and corporate clients.
3. Achieve a 30% growth in website traffic.

This rebrand will position Forever Flowers as a leader in the luxury floral market, combining timeless beauty with sustainability.
