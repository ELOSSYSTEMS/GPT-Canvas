---

## DECORATE Tier Job
Allow the customer to **make a confident aesthetic statement** without entering a service relationship.

DECORATE pages must:
- signal taste and restraint
- increase presence and weight
- feel deliberate, not exploratory

DECORATE pages must never:
- feel casual
- feel experimental
- feel instructional

---

## Allowed Behaviors
- Authority through selection
- Reduced choice sets
- Visual primacy
- Material honesty

## Forbidden Behaviors
- Try-level reassurance
- Custom/service framing
- Emotional identity language
- Urgency or persuasion

---

## Sets / Living Art Collection

### Job
Present fewer, heavier decisions that feel intentional.

### Required Sections
- Title
- One-line framing statement
- Reduced product grid (larger cards)

### Forbidden
- Filters beyond essentials
- Bestseller or popularity cues

---

## Set PDP

### Job
Establish scale, presence, and material authority immediately.

### Required Sections (Order Locked)
1. Single authoritative hero image
2. Name + price
3. Add to cart
4. Scale authority block (dimensions, placement)
5. Materials block
6. Gallery (below authority)

### Forbidden
- Multiple hero images
- Lifestyle storytelling copy
- Service language

---

## Portfolio – Decorate

### Job
Demonstrate taste through proof, not explanation.

### Required
- Image grid
- Minimal labels only

### Forbidden
- Case-study narratives
- Long captions

---

## Materials & Preservation

### Job
Demonstrate craftsmanship without spectacle.

### Required
- Vertical step list
- Myth vs truth table

### Forbidden
- Emotional reassurance
- Sustainability grandstanding

---

## Journal / Design Notes

### Job
Reinforce authority and taste over time.

### Required
- Featured article
- Article list

### Constraints
- Editorial tone only
- Observational, not persuasive

### Forbidden
- Product links
- Conversion CTAs

---

## QA Checklist (Fail If Any True)
- More than one hero image above the fold
- Decorative metaphors dominate
- Product links inside editorial content
- Service or customization implied
- Emotional language outweighs factual tone
