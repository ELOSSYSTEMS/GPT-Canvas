**FOREVER FLORALS — VISUAL FINGERPRINT: "SOFT STRUCTURE"**

---

## 🌺 Visual Identity Name:
**Soft Structure**

---

## ✨ Brand Essence:
Forever Florals occupies a unique space between decay and bloom, nostalgia and modernity, silence and texture. "Soft Structure" is the visual embodiment of that tension: a still-life world that treats every bouquet as a timeless artifact.

**Core Theme:**
*"Textural Radiance in Still Time"*


---

## 🎨 Color Philosophy: "Mineral Bloom"
> A palette that honors the muted integrity of dried florals, yet elevates them with light-infused elegance. Neither drab nor fresh.

### Primary Palette:
- **Ochre Dust**: grounded gold without shine
- **Plum Smoke**: softened violet-gray
- **Oxide Rose**: faded blush with rust undertones
- **Paprika Ash**: burnt coral midtone
- **Powdered Indigo**: antique blue-gray

### Accent Highlights:
- Chalk white (matte, not bleached)
- Patina brass
- Raw linen/beige bone

### Texture Behavior:
- Surfaces should **absorb** light, not reflect
- Matte ceramics, weathered plaster, porous stone

---

## 🛏️ Spatial Philosophy: "Air Between Things"
> Every composition must *breathe*. No clutter, no visual noise.

### Spatial Guidelines:
- One bouquet per scene — always centered
- Max 1–2 accessories: stoneware mug, antique scissors, old book
- Surfaces: rustic wood, poured concrete, unfinished oak
- Backgrounds: plaster walls, soft linen drapes, bare shelves

### Rule of Presence:
> The bouquet should feel *chosen*, not arranged.


---

## 💡 Light Logic: "Diffused Intimacy"

### Direction:
- Natural light only, side-cast (left or right window)

### Tone:
- Warm undertone, early morning or golden hour
- No harsh shadows, only gradations

### Mood:
> The lighting should suggest *time* and *stillness*, not production or performance.


---

## 🎭 Mood Signature: "Silent Luxury"
> The image shouldn't scream. It should *hum quietly*. 

- No visible branding/logos
- Props must feel like they live there, not styled
- Tone is contemplative, almost meditative
- Vignettes over wide shots

---

## 🏠 Environment Architecture

### Interior Type:
- Modern rustic / Belgian minimalism / Mediterranean silence

### Room Settings:
- Living Room: Worn armchair, heavy curtain, stone planter
- Kitchen: Earth-tone ceramics, matte cabinetry
- Entryway: Reclaimed bench, framed antique sketch

### Visual Tropes to Avoid:
- Scandinavian whitewash
- Boho overload
- Floral wallpaper / shabby chic
