## Purpose:
Unifies passive and structured journaling into one cognitive reflection system that adjusts based on user mode, intent, and recursion triggers.

---

## Modes of Operation:

### 1. **Passive Mode (Scribe)**
- Captures raw thoughts
- No structure, no feedback
- Used for stream-of-consciousness, late-night ideation, emotional discharge

### 2. **Structured Mode (Self-Journaler)**
- Prompts include:
  - Morning: gratitude, goals, tasks, appointments
  - Evening: review, reflection, lessons
- Includes task carryover and consistency tracking

### 3. **Meta Mode (System Reflection)**
- Asks:
  - “What changed in your pattern?”
  - “What belief collapsed or calcified today?”

---

## Adaptive Switching Engine:
- Shifts between modes based on:
  - User’s emotional state (via entropy/tone mapping)
  - System prompt (via recursion alerts)
  - Time of day / routine markers

---

## Additional Features:
- Auto-tags for pattern repetition
- Recursion triggers to the RJF when contradiction is detected
- Visual timeline of mood, language, and clarity drift over time
