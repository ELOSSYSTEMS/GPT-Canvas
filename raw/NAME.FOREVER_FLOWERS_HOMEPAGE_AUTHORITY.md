# FOREVER_FLOWERS_HOMEPAGE_AUTHORITY.md

## 0. Status
**Canonical.** This document governs the homepage permanently.  
The homepage is the most failure-prone page; this file exists to prevent creep.

---

## 1. Purpose
Define the homepage as a routing instrument, not a persuasion surface, aligned to Israeli luxury behavior.

The homepage must:
- reduce cognitive load
- normalize a first step
- route users cleanly

It must not:
- attempt to convert skeptics by explanation
- act as a brochure
- act as a blog

---

## 2. Homepage Job Description (One Sentence)
**The homepage orients and routes; it does not sell.**

---

## 3. Allowed Sections (Exact)
The homepage may include only these sections, in this order.

### 3.1 Header
- Logo
- Primary nav
- Cart

**Constraints**
- Navigation must remain minimal.
- No promotional banners.

### 3.2 Hero (Single Sentence)
- One sentence that answers: **“Is this appropriate for my home?”**

**Constraints**
- No second line.
- No paragraph.
- No claims that require proof.

### 3.3 “How People Usually Start” (Tier Reveal)
- Three calm cards:
  - TRY
  - DECORATE
  - CUSTOMIZE

Each card contains:
- Label
- One sentence (descriptive, not expressive)

**Constraints**
- No arrows.
- No buttons.
- No icons.
- No instruction text.

### 3.4 Home vs Business Gateway
- Two equal cards:
  - For the Home
  - For Businesses

Each card contains:
- Label
- One line description
- One CTA (text style)

**Constraints**
- Equal weight.
- No biasing language.

### 3.5 Featured Collections
- Up to two modules:
  - Bouquets
  - Sets

Each module contains:
- Title
- Grid preview
- “View all” link

**Constraints**
- No discount messaging.
- No “best seller” hype.

### 3.6 Trust Anchor
- One proof line only.

**Constraints**
- No stack of badges.
- No third-party logos unless unavoidable.

### 3.7 Footer
- Utility links

---

## 4. Forbidden Sections (Auto-Reject)
The homepage must never include:
- Testimonials
- Reviews widgets
- Long-form “About” story
- Process explanation
- Before/after narratives
- Promotions and countdowns
- Blog feed
- Social media feed
- "Why us" feature grids
- Multiple trust badges

Reason: These add noise and collapse luxury perception.

---

## 5. Copy Rules (Homepage-Specific)

### 5.1 Tone
- Calm
- Descriptive
- Specific

### 5.2 Prohibited Patterns
- Urgency
- Self-expression language ("my", "who I am")
- Emotional prompting ("you deserve")
- Budget framing

### 5.3 Sentence Constraints
- Prefer 6–12 words where possible.
- Avoid metaphors.
- Avoid questions.

---

## 6. Interaction Rules

### 6.1 CTA Style
- Text links preferred.
- No oversized buttons except "View collection".

### 6.2 Motion
- Minimal.
- No distracting animations.

### 6.3 Scrolling
- The page must feel complete within 1–2 scrolls.

---

## 7. QA Checklist (Pass/Fail)

A homepage build is rejected if any of the following are true:
- More than one sentence in hero
- Any testimonial present
- Any process explanation visible
- Any promotional banner exists
- Any icons appear in tier cards
- Any card contains more than one sentence
- Any section is added that is not listed in Allowed Sections

---

## 8. Change Control
Any proposed homepage change must include:
- the section being changed
- the justification in terms of effort/certainty/dignity
- proof it does not increase noise

Only Tamar Kay may approve exceptions.
