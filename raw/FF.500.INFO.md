**Forever Flowers – Info Page Structure**

**Tone & Purpose:**
A single, elegant page that answers key questions, builds brand trust, and offers contact—all without breaking the aesthetic. Inspired by RH and Habitat: minimal, honest, and quiet in tone.

**Sections:**

1. **Our Story**
- 3–5 sentence origin story
- Focus on craftsmanship, preservation technique, and emotional resonance
- No founder spotlight, just brand voice

2. **FAQ Highlights**
- ~5 most critical Q&As
- Format: bold question, calm one-line answer
- Topics: longevity, care, delivery timeline, returns, customization

3. **Shipping & Returns**
- Clear, confident phrasing
- Bulletless, paragraph-style: “We ship within 2–4 business days…”
- Emphasize handcrafted nature and seasonal exclusivity

4. **Contact Us**
- Simple form or email address
- Optional WhatsApp for premium support
- No social links unless branded and minimal

**Design Notes:**
- No images except a subtle background or divider motif
- Text-aligned to center or grid-left, depending on language direction
- White or neutral background, muted serif typography

This page exists to reduce customer hesitation without disrupting the visual flow of the brand.
