# Forever Flowers Rebuild Phases

## Phase 1: Foundation Setup
- Duplicate the Prestige theme
- Install fresh, no demo content
- Rename theme: "FF Rebuild"
- Remove all collection grids, product recommendations
- Delete any promo or urgency sections

## Phase 2: Minimal Structure & Navigation
- Create top navigation:
  - לבית
  - למתנה
  - חד-פעמי
- Build three landing pages:
  - לבית: explain the annual journey
  - למתנה: explain the three-date concept
  - חד-פעמי: simple page with three curated items
- Create two distinct collections:
  - Home styling collection (for the two tiers)
  - Gifting collection (for the gifting offer)

## Phase 3: Custom Section Creation
- Build custom sections:
  - Decision Split (for homepage if needed)
  - Fairness Block (clear delivery expectations)
  - Price Framing Block (₪/day with annual total)
  - Gifting Date Selector
  - Tier Overview (for home styling tiers)
- Assign custom templates:
  - Home Styling PDP template (two options)
  - Gifting PDP template (one option)
  - Single curated product template (for חד-פעמי)

## Phase 4: Content & Messaging
- Write all copy in Hebrew:
  - Minimal, confident, no “subscription” language
  - איך זה עובד לבית: החלטה אחת, ריענון לפי הצורך
  - איך זה עובד למתנה: שלושה רגעים, אנחנו מטפלים בהכל
  - Delivery clarity: "מגיע בתוך חמישה ימי עסקים"
- Prepare “Coming Soon” campaign:
  - Single image ad (elegant, minimal)
  - Caption hinting: “Forever begins soon” or “הפרחים של השנה מגיעים”
  - Ad objective: wide reach, no CTA

## Phase 5: Social & Marketing Reset
- Archive old social content that doesn’t fit new tone
- First new post: calm interior shot, caption: “אנחנו מתחילים מחדש”
- Pause current ad sets
- Launch “Coming Soon” campaign

## Phase 6: Final Checks & Launch
- Test full customer flow (gifting and home styling)
- Ensure all links and pages are clear, minimal, aligned
- On February 1st, switch theme to new FF Rebuild
- Announce quietly: website update
- First post-launch ad: introduce the forever journey—focus on “הכל מסודר לשנה”

---

Each phase builds toward a minimal, confident experience. By launch, the focus is on calm decision-making—whether for gifting or home styling—without ever needing to say “subscription.”
